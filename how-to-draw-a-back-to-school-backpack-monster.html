Today, Jack and I are learning how to draw this funny folding surprise backpack monster! We hope you have a lot of fun following along and changing your monsters.

~Sharpie (or something to draw with)
~Paper (we use marker paper)
~Markers to color with (we use Bianyo)
~Colored pencils (sometimes we also use Prismacolor colored pencils)
