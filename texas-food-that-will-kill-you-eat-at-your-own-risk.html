TEXAS FOOD THAT CAN KILL YOU!

1. HYPNOTIC DONUT
ADDRESS: 9007 Garland Rd, Dallas, TX 75218, United States
INTERVIEWEE: Bruce

CANADIAN HEALTHCARE: Take a Long John-shaped donut & dip in maple icing and evenly spread it across the top. Add a strip of bacon.

GUERILLA WARFARE: Roll out chocolate dough and cut into a rectangle. Mash a banana on top of it. Top with peanuts. Fold over into a square and chop the dough. Fold it over again, forming a log and roll it out to smooth. Cut into even pieces. Pound them to flatten into circular shapes. Place in the proofer for 10 minutes. Remove from the proofer and fry. Glaze. Decorate with banana and chocolate swirl

2. MAPLE LEAF DINER
ADDRESS: 12817 Preston Rd #129, Dallas, TX 75230, United States
INTERVIEWEE: Mike

SOUTHERN POUTINE: Cut fried chicken. Add a bit of vinegar powder on french fries.		 Assembly: in a bowl, add fries, add white cheddar cheese curds on top, add more fries, add more cheese, add two scoops of gravy, add fried chicken. Drizzle on more gravy and top with green onions.

STEAK WAFFLE: Bread steaks and fry. Grill onions and green peppers. Grill Canadian bacon
Assembly: Place waffle on a plate. Top with two pieces of fried steak. Add white gravy. Add onions and peppers. Place a second waffle on top. Place two more pieces of fried steak. Add more gravy. Add more onions and peppers. Skewer with a stick. Add 3 slices of Canadian bacon. Add two cream puffs.		 Gravy: spices, flour, cream, black pepper, garlic, onion powde

💸 PRICE: Southern Poutine - $10.99 USD		 Steak Waffle - 16.99 USD

3. THE FAT SHACK
ADDRESS: 6205 Coit Rd #364, Plano, TX 75024, United States
WEBSITE: https://www.fatshack.com/our-story
INTERVIEWEE: Karman

DEEP-FRIED OREOS: Dip in batter & deep fry.

THE FAT SHACK: Toast bread & lay out french fries. Cut chickens in half and place 3 halves on the French fries. On the other side lay out mozzarella sticks. Place onion rings in the middle. Place jalapeño poppers in the middle. Add cheesesteak. Drizzle on honey jalapeño sauce. Stuff it. Wrap it. Cut it in half
