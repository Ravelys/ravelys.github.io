1) 3" C-CHANNEL (3 pieces, base) - 12" long
2) 1 1/2" Angle ( 2 pieces, base) - 12 1/2" long
3) 3" Angle ( 1 piece, welded on top of base) 11 3/4" long
4) 2" Angle (6 pieces, gussets) 2" long
5) 1" pipe (2 pieces, risers) 9" long
6) 1" pipe (2 pieces, cross pieces) 4 1/2" long
