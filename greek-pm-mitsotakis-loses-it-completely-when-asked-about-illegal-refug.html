"There has been overwhelming evidence and you keep denying and lying. This is like narcissistic abuse. Why are you not honest? Now I have a say and yes, I do cruel, barbarian pushbacks", Ingeborg Beugel said and Mitsotakis, being used το flattering questions by greek journalists, lost it completely.
