Liesbeth and Angelique Raeven are identical twins who together form the artist duo L.A. Raeven. They share 100% of their DNA and do everything together from sharing their food right down to the last crumb. Now one of them wants to leave to lead her own life alone but it’s not going down well with the other sister. This unique absolute documentary follows these sisters’ journey.
