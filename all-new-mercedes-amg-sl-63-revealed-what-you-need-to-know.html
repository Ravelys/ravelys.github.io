Introducing the all-new Mercedes-AMG SL 63!

It’s a replacement for the AMG GT Roadster, and it’s set to go head-to-head with class-leading drop-tops such as the BMW 8 Series Convertible & Porsche 911 Cabriolet! So, does it have what it takes?

Well for starters, the Mercedes designers have clearly cranked up the aggressive looks in comparison to the outgoing GT Roadster. The new SL rolls-up sporting an absolutely huge grille along with some seriously pointy headlights. The impressive looks continue once you take a step inside, too, with the dash dominated with a huge infotainment screen just like you’ll find in the new C-Class & S-Class.

In terms of performance, the range-topper is housing a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 under the bonnet, to deliver an awesome 585hp & 800Nm of torque! It’ll also hit 60mph in an awesome 3.6 seconds!

Naturally, it won’t come cheap, with the new SL coming in with a starting price of £100,000! So, is it worth it? Or should you choose the 911 Cab instead? Let us know in the COMMENTS!
