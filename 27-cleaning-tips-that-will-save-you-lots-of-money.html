BREATH-TAKING CLEANING TIPS YOU’VE NEVER SEEN BEFORE



To make an effective toilet-bowl cleaner mix some washing up liquid, baking soda, and citric acid and blend them together well. Then, transfer in a silicon mold, let them harden and dip one every other day to clean your toilet.

When sweeping the floor, always spray some water on your dustpan to prevent little particles from flying over and making a mess.

You can make your own bathroom by mixing some vinegar with warm water and washing up liquid.

To remove mold from the crevices in your bathroom mix some baking soda with bleach, dip cotton pads inside and then place them on the affected areas. Let them sit in place for a few minutes and finally remove.
