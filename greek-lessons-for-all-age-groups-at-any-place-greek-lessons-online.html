Greek Lessons OnLine is the first Greek OnLine School, operating since 2012. Greek LOL offers greek language courses to students of all age groups from all over the world. Kids and adults join the Greek LOL virtual classrooms from the comfort of their home and learn Greek through highly interactive materials and live communication with their classmates and their teacher! Greek LOL is a very special "place" for students of greek origin and Philhellenes to learn Greek!
