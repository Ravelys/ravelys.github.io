TIPS FOR A PERFECT SUMMER

Summer is here and we are ready to share with you cool summer ideas to make your holidays awesome! It doesn’t matter where do you usually spend your holidays, these summer lifehacks will change your life! You will find cool ideas on how to repair flip flops using a paper clip, how to freeze water, how to save your valuables using a jar and tennis ball and more!
You should always have aloe vera gel with you in summer as it’s a magical treatment for such skin problems as dry skin and sunburn. Using a blender mix aloe vera gel, cucumbers, water, and lemon juice. This mixture perfectly hydrates your skin. You should always apply sunscreen when you relax at the beach. In the case of sunscreen, use aloe vera ice cubes to help your skin. Take an ice tray and pour aloe vera, freeze. And apply aloe ice cubes for pain relieve and to moisturize and heal your skin.
Use lemon juice to naturally lighten your hair. Spray lemon juice on your hair and leave for 3 hours when you spend time at the beach. You will have a perfect result after 3 sunny days. Watch our video and learn how to make sunscreen stick in 5 minutes.
Learn how to cope with heat stroke. Follow first aid steps: find a shadow and lay down, elevate the feet, apply the damp cloth on neck and chest, and give cold water to the person. If necessary, call an emergency.
Reuse plastic bottles and make a cool and cheap beach bag in 5 minutes. Cut plastic bottles and attach them using a rope. Watch the full tutorial.
Use a ziplock bag to protect your phone from sand when you relax at the beach. Use cupcake liners to protect your drinks from insects.
