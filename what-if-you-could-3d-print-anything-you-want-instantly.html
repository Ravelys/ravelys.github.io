3D Printing technology allows us to print toys, furniture, moulds for making jewelry and much more. It creates three-dimensional objects from a solid digital file. Does it mean that potentially we could print out anything we want on a 3D printer? Like a real car, a bed to sleep on, or even a house? Let’s imagine what’d happen if we could print out the whole world on a 3D printer!
