Glue Collaboration (Helsinki, Finland) is the leader in real-time virtual collaboration software.

In Glue, teams collaborate in persistent, feature-rich and fully customizable shared virtual spaces. With world-class immersive 3D graphics, spatialized audio design and AI-powered avatars, Glue is a place where communication is natural and effortless, and where users experience a true sense of presence.
