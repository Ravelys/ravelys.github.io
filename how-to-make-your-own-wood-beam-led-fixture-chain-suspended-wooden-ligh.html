How to make your own Wooden Beam Lighting suspended fixture
This whole diy project aims to show how you can make your own handcraft diy wood project with as little tools as possible.

The beam was pre-ordered with custom sizes (155cm * 12cm * 12cm)
All used tools and materials can be found at your local tools and accessories store.

The Led was mounted on aluminum rail to minimize the frequency of repairs and changes as well as for the balast.

This Pendant light took around 5 days to finish from start to hanging.
