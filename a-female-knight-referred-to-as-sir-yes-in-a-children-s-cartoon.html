Google apparently accepted her resignation even though she never resigned! So obviously, Google are woke in name only. Knight: (in the Middle Ages) a man who served his sovereign or lord as a mounted soldier in armour. Sir: used as a title before the forename of a knight or baronet. Okay, so clearly these are masculine terms, but a recent children’s TV show that my young children are currently watching and enjoying immensely are trying to turn these definitions on their head.
