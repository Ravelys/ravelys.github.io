This video is about a very useful and advance English structure “IN VIEW OF” AND “IN VIEW OF THE FACT THAT”
Let’s see some examples:
Uski garibi ko dekhte huye, maine use kuchh paise de diye.
Uske bure behavior ko dhyan me rakhte huye, maine use invite nahi kiya.
HINDI PAHCHAAN:
……………………………
Dekhte huye,
Dhyan me rakhte huye
ENGLISH SITUATION:
………………………………..
Keeping in mind,
Taking into consideration
Structure1:
………………
[In view of + noun, sentence]
Ex: In view of his bad behavior I didn’t invite him,
In view of my friend’s attitude, I don’t talk to him.

Structure 2:
…………………
[In view of the fact that + sentence, sentence]
Ex: Is baat ko dekhte huye ki wah bimaar hai, hame use disturb nahi karna chahiye.
In view of the fact that he is ill, we should not disturb him.
