This episode we are back at Waroora Station. We stay at Stevens which is beach camping right in the middle of Warroora coast . To stay here you’ll need a Offgrid, self sufficient setup and its 8$ per night.
In this episode we go Fishing, surfing, snorkelling and spearfishing. We also dabble with a catch and cook! I hope you guys enjoy.
