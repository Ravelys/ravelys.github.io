Egor Vasilyevich Kliuka (Russian: Егор Васильевич Клюка (born 15 June 1995) is a Russian volleyball player of Belarusian descent. He is part of the Russia men’s national volleyball team.[1] He competed at the 2015 European Championship. On club level, he plays for Russian club Zenit Saint Petersburg.
