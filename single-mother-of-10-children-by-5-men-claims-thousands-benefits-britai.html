The documentary examining the lives of people on benefits focuses on those who have large families, including 49-year-old Mandy, who has 10 children by five fathers and claims thousands of pounds a year in benefits. She lives in Hastings, East Sussex, with three of her offspring and is unrepentant about her situation, saying her goal is to have 50 grandchildren.
