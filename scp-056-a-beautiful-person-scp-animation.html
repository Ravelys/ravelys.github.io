Dr Bob brings you SCP Foundation Euclid Class object, SCP-056 A Beautiful Person Animation.

SCP 056 is a being of variable size, gender, and appearance, which changes in response to the environment around it, especially in regards to living and sentient beings. The most common form SCP056 takes is of a handsome man in his middle twenties, dressed in a garb of similar appearance to that of the personnel guarding it but of a higher quality and aesthetic value
