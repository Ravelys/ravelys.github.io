Known for his futuristic, dub techno sound, Stuttgart’s LDS releases his 1st full EP on Monnom Black to shine some light during these cold isolated times. LDS brings forth his vision during an uncertain time for both nightlife and the world whilst humanity stands at the hinge of merging with machines.
