Peace and freedom have never felt more real, and Farid Noori is not keeping it to himself.
Through the bicycle, Farid tasted freedom for the first time. He now fundamentally believes that this simple, noble act of pedalling may be the very thing to liberate his brothers and sisters and rewrite the story of Afghanistan, a place he still calls home.

Farid Noori grew up both in Afghanistan, and as a refugee in Pakistan. He recalls a childhood without any play or sports opportunities. When he came to the US as a student, Farid discovered cycling and immediately fell in love with it.
