On November 22, the Shrine held the first event in our new monthly series, Evenings with the Merciful Jesus. Led by Sister Donata Farbaniec and Sister Gaudia Skass of the Congregation of the Sisters of Our Lady of Mercy, each evening of the 22 of the month features a talk, adoration with praise and worship, confession, refreshments, and fellowship.
