In contrast to instant gratification — delayed gratification is when you acknowledge the value in something and act patiently to receive it. You must wait in the Spirit, work in the flesh, and watch how God will elevate you higher than you ever thought possible. The cost to get it includes submission, strategy, surrender, and suffering. Your better isn’t far off, but what price are you willing to pay to get there?
