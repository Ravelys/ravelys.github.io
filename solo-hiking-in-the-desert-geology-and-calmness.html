first video from a series of my Hiking Videos in the Negev desert.

in this video I decided to document the desert, and my hiking through the desert landscape, in a way that conveys the power of the desert in a more touching way than just a photo.

At the end of the video you can see some landscape photos I took during the hike, and a location map of the trail.
