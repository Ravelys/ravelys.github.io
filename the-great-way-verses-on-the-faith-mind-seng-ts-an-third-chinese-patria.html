Hsin-hsin Ming, commonly known as "Verses on the Faith-Mind" by Seng-ts’an, Third Zen Patriarch. This is a class short Zen scripture. It really expresses a profound viewpoint on reality. It is short - but many people contemplate it for many years, finding deep inspiration for their daily practices and way of life.

The text can be found here: http://www.mendosa.com/way.html
Translated By Richard B. Clarke
