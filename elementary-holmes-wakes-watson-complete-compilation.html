What most people don’t know is that the Holmes-Waking-Up-Watson running gag is in fact canon, i.e, it was taken straight from the original Sherlock Holmes stories. I can remember at least three events where Holmes woke Watson up in the books. The Speckled Band, The Abbey Grange, and The Man With The Twisted Lip. However, Elementary does make a bit too much of a joke out of it.
