For those who work in big corporations, the antics that occur at FCT, a recently acquired subsidiary of URBM, will be all too familiar. In "Standards of Ethical Conduct", you’ll follow the story of Heff, a cubicle-dweller who finds himself in conflict with FCT’s nightmarish code of conduct after an unfortunate incident at the annual company Halloween party. Last Comic Standing semi-finalist Chuck Roy co-stars as Heff’s stoner roommate, a man pursuing a very different sort of American Dream. If you like an office comedy like "Office Space" or an office show like "The Office", you’ll dig this.
