A real hurricane hit Moscow on October 22, 2021. The wind blew with gusts of up to 20 meters per second attacked not only the outskirts, but also hit the very heart of the capital - the Kremlin. See how easily a raging hurricane demolishes the scaffolding installed at the Kremlin wall. All this happened in front of numerous passers-by. It’s good that no one was hurt. The hurricane easily picks up and demolishes almost all light and loose structures. The wind in some districts of Moscow began to carry away garbage cans and fell trees. One of them fell onto the playground. Several more trees landed on parked cars.
