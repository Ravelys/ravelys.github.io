World of Warcraft Patch 8.3 Visions of N’zoth opened the doors on the Ny’alotha raid and revealed it’s grand expansion-ending cinematic… and people don’t like it. Is this a fitting end to Battle for Azeroth? Join us as we go over everything that happened in another deep-dive cinematic analysis and ask… did N’zoth just get done dirty?
