The iPhone/iPad application to let you discover new music based on your tastes.

iTunes: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/fmfinder-music-discovery-recommendations/id569215761?ls=1&mt=8

Music recommendations based on:
- Current playback item on iPod app
- Entire iPod library: playlists, artists, albums, or all songs
- Last.fm top songs
- iTunes top songs
- iTunes top music videos

Filter, search for songs by name or by artist, on your library or on last.fm, iTunes.

Similar songs are shown in a beautiful graph with the similar relationship represented by the line connecting them. Switch to similar artists is within a thumb.

Watch related YouTube videos of the song, or artists, read wiki information about the song, artist.

Download the music audio or video you love on iTunes.
