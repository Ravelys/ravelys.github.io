Music recommendations based on:
- Current playback item on iPod app
- Entire iPod library: playlists, artists, albums, or all songs
- Last.fm top songs
- iTunes top songs
- iTunes top music videos

Filter, search for songs by name or by artist, on your library or on last.fm, iTunes.
