As eCommerce entrepreneurs that are building clothing brands, it’s key that you know what to expect as you design for different product lines. Dye sublimation is great for vibrant colorways and can really help your designs stand out as you merchandise your line. Unfortunately sublimation printing doesn’t work the same on fabrics such as cotton or cotton blends. Throughout this video you will learn why that is, and how to create a game plan for success as you expand your clothing line.
