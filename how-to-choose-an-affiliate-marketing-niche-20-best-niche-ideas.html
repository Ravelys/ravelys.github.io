How to Find a Niche - 8 Considerations:

1. Interest & Passion - Do you have interest and are you passionate about your niche?

2. Search Volume - Is there enough search volume for the main keywords you will target?

3. Affiliate Programs & Products - Are there a lot of affiliate programs and affiliate products to promote?

4. Product Reviews - Can you write product reviews about different products in your niche?

5. Product Guides - Can you write product guides and top-product lists?

6. Customers & Pain Points - Do you understand your target customers and their pain points?

7. Competition - Are there large competitors and can you compete with your website?

8. Interest Level - Is there enough interest level that there are fans on social media websites?
