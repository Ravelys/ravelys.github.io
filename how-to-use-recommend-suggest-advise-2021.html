Is RECOMMEND ME correct? Is SUGGEST ME correct? Is ADVISE ME correct?

This is the #1 English mistake I hear from all student, including advanced students.

Today you’ll learn how the correct sentence structure of recommend, suggestion, advise.

First we’ll look at suggest and recommend grammar. They have the same sentence structure.

Next we’ll look at advise grammar, because it has a slightly different sentence structure.
