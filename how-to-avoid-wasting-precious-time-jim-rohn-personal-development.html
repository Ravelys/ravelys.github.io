In this Jim Rohn personal development video, he talks about how to stop wasting precious time when it comes to achieving your dreams.

In this video, Jim Rohn shares how you can avoid wasting precious time. He discusses the importance of self-discipline and managing your own internal dialogue.

He also talks about how to be grateful for what you have and stop focusing on what you don’t have.

Jim Rohn said, "You are the average of the five people you spend time with." If you want to increase your personal growth and success, it’s important to be around people who support those goals.
