Meaning of Eager : marked by strong interest or impatient desire; full of ENTHUSIASM

Examples:
1. The FBI was eager to prosecute the mobster for extortion and money laundering.
2. She waited in eager anticipation for Robert to arrive.
3. We look forward to your lecture with eager anticipation.
4. Haig was desperately eager for an affirmative answer.
5. The boy was eager for success.
