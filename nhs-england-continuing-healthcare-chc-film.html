This public information film is a guide for individuals and their families to NHS Continuing Healthcare and what to expect throughout the complex assessment process. There is also a version of this film which has been split up into bite-size sections describing the individual elements of the assessment process. For further information, individuals should contact their local Clinical Commissioning Group’s NHS Continuing Healthcare team.
