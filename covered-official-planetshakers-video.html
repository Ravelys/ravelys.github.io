We are so excited about our latest album release ‘This is Our Time’!

The album consists of 15 brand new tracks recorded at Planetshakers ‘Awakening’, Melbourne 2014. This album is a prophetic statement, not only over Planetshakers, but over your life- declaring that this is YOUR time to be all that God has called you to be!
