A review of how to fix lazy eye (amblyopia), specifically reviewing the treatment strategies used by eye doctors to improve vision and reduce strabismus. Oftentimes glasses, eye patching, atropine eye drops and eye exercises (vision therapy) or a combination will be used to help. In advanced cases eye surgery may still be needed.
