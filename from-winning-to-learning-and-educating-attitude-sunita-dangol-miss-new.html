Sunita Dangol won Miss Newa 2011 title. Soon, she tried her hand at coaching children preparing for Little Miss Newa beauty pageant. She also ran radio and TV shows. Later she got interested in Nepal Bhasa and hence she started to learn Nepal Bhasa lipi (script) in 2014. She then started to teach the script and even formed Callijatra, an informal group working to promote and preserve the language.
