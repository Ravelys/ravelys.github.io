22 STORE ASSISTANT INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
Q1. Tell me about yourself?
Q2. Why do you want to work for our company?
Q3. What can you bring to the role of a Store Assistant?
Q4. Tell me about a time you had to handle a dissatisfied customer?
Q5. What is more important, team or customer?
Q6. What does good customer service mean to you?
Q7. Tell me a time you experienced conflict with someone and how you resolved it?
Q8. Tell me a time when you went out of your way to help a customer?
Q9. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Q10. We take pride in providing excellent customer service. How would you deal with a customer complaint whilst working as a Store Assistant?
Q11. Tell me a time when you manage lots of tasks all at once?
Q12. What skills and qualities do you have that will help you become a great Store Assistant?
Q13. Tell me a time when you dealt with a stressful situation?
Q14. What would you bring to our team?
Q15. Explain a situation you were in where you successfully dealt with a difficult or frustrated customer?
Q16. What would you do if you didn’t get on with someone within the store team?
Q17. Tell me a time when you worked with other people as part of a team?
Q18. We are looking for a Store Assistant who is flexible and adaptable. Tell me a time when you demonstrated flexibility at work?
Q19. Why do you want to leave your job?
Q20. Where do you see yourself in 5 years’ time?
Q21. We are interviewing five other people for the job of Store Assistant – What makes you the standout candidate?
Q22. That’s the end of your Store Assistant job interview. Do you have questions for the panel?
