Let There Be Fire is one week away! To kick off the preorder, here is my tribute to the Maiden in Black from Demon’s Souls, and her quest to lull an ancient demon back to sleep. The music is a strange mix of Orchestral EDM and Alt Rock I’ve never really done before, taking the themes of DeS into a different soundscape than its own soundtrack. It’s the third single of many more to come from the new album!
