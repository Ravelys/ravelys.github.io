Week 1: Grounding Skills of 5 Senses, Movement, and Breathing
Week 2: Check-in Exercises of Keys, Environment, and Toys
Week 3: Warm Up Exercises
Week 4: Techniques for supporting client that may appear “stuck” clients
Week 5: Exercises to increase empathy for clients that seem “challenging"
Week 6: Resourcing Tools
Week 7: Internal vs External Work
Week 8: Main Exercises
Week 9: Anchoring
Week 10: Meaning Making Practices
Week 11: Check Out Exercises
Week 12: Closure

The short videos contain snippet of insight for you to spark greater momentum in all of the circles you lead, from group therapy and beyond.
