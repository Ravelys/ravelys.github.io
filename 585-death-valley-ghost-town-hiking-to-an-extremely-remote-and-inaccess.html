So I took a day off, and drove 45 minutes into my new "backyard," aka Death Valley, to explore an extremely remote and nearly inaccessible abandoned mine camp. Because it’s so hard to get here, the camp is relatively well preserved and full of interesting artifacts. What an adventure!
