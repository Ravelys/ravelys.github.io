Award-winning designer Roksanda Illincic has been at the forefront of the fashion world for over a decade. Now she brings her flawless style to Sotheby’s as guest curator of our upcoming sale Contemporary Curated (20 November London). Roksanda’s selection reflects her approach to fashion design, celebrating texture, contrast and particularly colour. Her highlights include works by Amy Sillman, Aleksandra Domanovic and Anthony Caro.
