Learn English: Learn jobs (occupation) and places of work (employment). In the first half of this video you will practice with Mark Kulek and in the second half, you will practice on your own. This will build your English speaking skills and fluency. Each conversation is illustrated and color coded for better understanding. This is an interactive listening and speaking video.
