The interdisciplinary approach – encompassing film, performance and experimental storytelling – of emerging designer Grace Emily Manning, is driven by environmentalism.

Here she tells us about her career journey, creative process, and sustainable approach to making. Taking waste products from the outdoors, she turns them into new materials for her projects, which are often inspired by nature itself.
