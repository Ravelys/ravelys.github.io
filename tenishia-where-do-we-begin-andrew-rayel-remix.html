Causing a dream to turn into reality, Tenishia’s debut album ‘Memory of a Dream’ gathers 8 years of collaboration. One of its many gems, and the official follow-up to ‘Point Of No Return’ and ‘As It Should’, is the emotional ‘Where Do We Begin’.

An eclectic affair, that walks the fine line of prog, trance and even touches the tips of house, is ‘Where Do We Begin’. Driven by lush piano, touching vocals and a strong melody, Tenishia once again shows to be able to touch our hearts.

Also moving us on the inside, is the rework of rising star Andrew Rayel. Being one of the most requested remixers of the moment, Tenishia snatched up the Moldavian producer for a retouch of ‘Where Do We Begin’. And the result is an absolute winner for the peak-time hours.
