The technique of making sandals from used motorcycle tires.
Used motorcycle tires that can be used to make sandals are large and wide motorcycle tires.
In this video I will make sandals from used motorcycle tires, but use a bisban to make it more attractive.
The learning steps are:
1. Cut a wide used motorcycle tire, cut according to the pattern
2. Make a sandal sling with a bisban
3. Make a hole in the face of the sandal, make a side incision where the sash is.
4. Attach the sash
