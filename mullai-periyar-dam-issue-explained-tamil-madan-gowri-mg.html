The state of Kerala and Tamil Nadu have been debating on the internet about the Mullai Periyar Dam over the past week after the Kerala floods. It is also a point of contention between Chennai and Kochi. Especially after the rains in Thekkady, Munnar, Mallapuram over the past week.
