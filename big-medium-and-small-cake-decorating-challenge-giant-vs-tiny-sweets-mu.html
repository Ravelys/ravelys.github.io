Big cake, medium cake and small cake. It’s good when you can eat all 3, but when you have to share, you don’t really want to stay with a tiny sweets. But the girls from the video will have to do this to become the participants of the big, medium and small cake decorating challenge. Everyone understands that decorating a giant cake is much more fun, because you can embody so many ideas and place all the sweets of the world there! But with a tiny cake, the decoration will be more difficult, because small things are so easy to spoil. But the talent of a decorator can be seen even on a small cake, because the judge will be the most attentive spectator and it is up to you to decide whose cake is the most beautiful!
