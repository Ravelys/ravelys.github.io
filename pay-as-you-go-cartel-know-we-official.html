One of the largest garage crews, Pay as You Go Cartel is notable as being the starting place for MCs Wiley, God’s Gift, Maxwell D, Breeze and Flow Dan, producer Target, and also UK underground music figureheads DJ Slimzee and Geeneus. Along with Heartless Crew and the So Solid Crew, Pay as You Go are recognised as pioneering influences on the grime sound.

Know We was their first track produced by Wiley and regarded as the Cartels most outstanding release.
