Learn how you can create an Ebook for free.

Let’s say you’ve a website and you want to get email ids of your visitors.

Now instead of asking them to subscribe to your website, you can give them an Ebook which will encourage them to subscribe to your email list.

So you can create your Ebook by following our 4 simple steps.

Let’s get started.

𝗧𝗮𝗯𝗹𝗲 𝗼𝗳 𝗰𝗼𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀 :

00:00 Intro

00:48 Create an account on Crello

01:27 Choose a design for your Ebook

02:09 Add content to the design

03:58 Create a new page from scratch


Step 1 : Create an account on Crello

Crello is the website which helps you to create your Ebook.

So to create account in Crello, click this link ➜ https://wl.tools/crello

Now click ‘Signup’ and fill the required details.

Once you’ve created an account you can go to the next step.

Step 2 : Choose a design for your Ebook

To choose a design, search for Ebook designs

Now pick a design you like and click on it.

Now you will be taken to a page where you can customize the design and see all the pages which come with the design.

Step 3 : Add content to the design

Now to customize your Ebook, you can add your own content in it.

Now if you want to replace text, just click on it and enter your own text.

Similarly you can change anything like image, background color etc.

Step 4 : Create a new page from scratch

To create a new page, click ‘New Page’

Now to add contents in it, you can select any item from the sidebar menu.

If you want to add text click on ‘Text’ and choose any style you want.

If you want to add an image, you can click ‘Photos’ & choose an image from the library

Or you can add your own image by clicking on ‘My Files’.

Now if you want to rearrange the pages, just drag the page in the order you want.

You can also duplicate the page by clicking the 3 dots option on the page and then click ‘Duplicate’ so that you can customize the content easily.

So now you’ve successfully created your Ebook.

Now just give a name to it and click ‘Download’.

You can also choose the format you want to download.

In the free version, you can only download 5 Ebooks per month.

If you want to download more than 5, you need to upgrade your plan on crello.

So that’s it guys.

This is how you can create an Ebook for free.
