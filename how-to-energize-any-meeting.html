Are you planning a meeting and want to avoid "death by powerpoint"? Do you want to energise your conference? In this video you can find out how to get your participating with a musical energiser session.

Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/musicalteambuilding/

TRANSCRIPT:
Imagine, if you could get 500 people at your next conference all making music together. I’m Neal Fullerton from musicalteambuilding.com and I’ve been working with conferences and events for international companies and organisations for 15 years. As a musician, I can tell you that making music with others is all about teamwork. It’s about listening to each other, communicating and playing your part.

Just focus on your part and you’ll see that as a team, we can make something pretty cool happen. Using music this way, we can transform your audience into participants. With everyone focusing on a shared task, and actually mastering the piece of music together you can create a really powerful team spirit. Why, we can even get the whole group singing together as one!

So whether you want an icebreaker to get everyone talking, an unusual team building activity or an energiser after lunch to re-focus everyone - musical team building is a great way to do it. Click on the link now to visit the website and let’s make your next conference a huge success.
