He is the ultimate high end LA plastic surgery junkie and he might give me a run for my money…” PoisonEyez added: “I’ve been cut, pulled, pumped, sucked and stitched back up by the best plastic surgeons in the world." Justin will learn more about Poison’s body transformation and how much he has spent on his look. However, will there be any rivalry between these two Ken Dolls?
