Why is China’s "Meritocracy" appropriate for the world trends, the national conditions of China and Chinese people? Daniel A. Bell is Dean of the School of Political Science and Public Administration at Shandong University and professor at Tsinghua University (Schwarzman College and Department of Philosophy).
