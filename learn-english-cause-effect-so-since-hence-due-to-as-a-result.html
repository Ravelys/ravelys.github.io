Learn exactly how to use important words to explain why or how something happened in English. In this English vocabulary class, I will teach you key vocabulary to show cause and effect including words like “so”, “because of”, “since”, “as a result”, “consequently”, “hence”, “thus”, and “therefore”. You may know these words already, but I will show you exactly when to use each one and how to use each one.
