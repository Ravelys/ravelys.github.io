Atheists often respond to the cosmic fine-tuning argument by appealing to the multiverse. If our universe is one of a vast (possibly infinite) array of universes, perhaps a life-permitting universe isnt so improbable after all. But if atheists can appeal to the multiverse to answer cosmic fine-tuning, cant theists appeal to the multiverse to answer the problem of evil?
