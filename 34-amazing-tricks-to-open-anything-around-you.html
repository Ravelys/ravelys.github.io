Experiments with magnets are the best ones! They are not only used around the home but also perfect subjects for simple science experiments. The first experiment is incredibly easy and cool! Place three glasses in a row and fill the first one with water, the second one with vegetable oil and the third one with syrup. Place several paper clips in each glass. Place a piece of glass over the glasses and take a magnet and place over each glass. Check out how differently paper clips move. Have you ever seen ferrofluid? You can make this experiment using two simple ingredients: vegetable oil and printer ink. In a bowl, mix printer ink with a small amount of vegetable oil and stir.
