Sep.22 – Federal Reserve Chairman Jerome Powell says the central bank will review the rules around trading and asset holdings for reserve bank presidents after trading activity by Boston Fed President Eric Rosengren and Dallas’s Robert Kaplan were scrutinized for the potential conflict of interest.
