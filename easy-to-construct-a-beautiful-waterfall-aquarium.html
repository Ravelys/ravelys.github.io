Do you want to improve your yard? Your yard may look lifeless, the grass is never trimmed, and there are a lot of insects. Therefore, I want to share with you how to decorate your yard such as planting more trees, building a waterfall aquarium.

Watch the video to see the final result!
The materials are simple and easy to find:
__
Cement, sand, reinforcement, bricks, foam, PVC pipes, plastic pipes, gravel
Brush, gas torch, pump
Paint color: black blue, white, orange earth.
