Nezu wants to minimize the risk of talking about attacks on UA/possibly accusing someone of being the traitor. He knows he can’t really stop the talk at all, but if some teachers haven’t learnt enough sign language yet then in the classroom sign language can not be used. However if Mic was teaching the class, they would be able to use sign language since he understands and will be able to point out if any traitor discussion is going on.
