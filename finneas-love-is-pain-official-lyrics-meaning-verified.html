“Love is Pain” is the latest hit from 24-year-old producer, songwriter, and musician FINNEAS. The song is featured on his debut album ‘Optimist’ and has already racked up over 3 million Spotify plays. FINNEAS says he felt motivated to write the song because there are a lot of songs about falling in love and breaking up but a shortage of songs on the difficulty of maintaining a healthy relationship.
