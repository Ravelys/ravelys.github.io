As soon as objects around us become part of our everyday routine, we stop wondering why they were created this way a long time ago. Sometimes we stop fully understanding the purpose of all the details of everyday items. For example, the holes on coffee stirrers are there for a reason, it’s not just a random idea of a designer. So much though was put into designing them! Let’s take a look at 76 details of these simple objects that have a hidden purpose.
