Read by seven year old Nathan, SO MUCH by British author Trish Cooke is a book that celebrates Father’s and is a window in the joys of family in Afro-Caribbean setting! It’s great book to read to celebrate Father’s Day!

Illustrator: SO MUCH is illustrated by Helen Oxenbury.
