ASOS, a leading online fashion retailer, transformed its platform from a monolithic, on-premises e-commerce system to a microservices platform running on Microsoft Azure. The rapidly growing company has also accelerated development of innovative mobile apps and features to quickly target new markets and stay on top of consumer and technology trends.
