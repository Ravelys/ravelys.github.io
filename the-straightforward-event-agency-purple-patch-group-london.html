Purple Patch Group are a London based event management team committed to delivering effective events. With over 25 years of experience in planning, designing, and managing events for clients across the world we’re a well-established event agency.

Purple Patch Group was born out of a need to create and manage events from start to finish, that deliver our clients goals. We specialise in creating events that run smoothly throughout – using our experienced team who have served 50,000 delegates since we started in 2004.
