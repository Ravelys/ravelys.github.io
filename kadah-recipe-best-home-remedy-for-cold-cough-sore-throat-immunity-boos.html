2Kadah Recipe: Kadha is a traditional Indian drink. Kadah is a natural immunity booster & best home remedy for fever, cold, cough & for sore throat. It is a rich source of antioxidants. You can make it at home using simple & few ingredients. This is just a perfect & easy recipe for you!

NOTE :

1. Don’t add too much spices, excess of any ingredients can cause heartburn

2. Use Organic honey for best results

3. Serve Hot for best results




Ingredients for Kadah

1. 750 ml water
2.1/2 teaspoon of Fennel seeds(saunf)
3.1/2 teaspoon of Ajwain
4.5-7 Pepper
5. 3 Cardamoms
6.3 Cloves
7.Small pieces of Cinnamon
8.1/2 inch of ginger
9.1 teaspoon of honey
10.Few leaves of mint( pudina)
