This was the most requested workout eveeeer! I know it’s super hard to train your back muscles at home, if you don’t have any equipment. But if we get a little creative .. we CAN make it work!

Training your back is super duper important, because everything in our body should be balanced :) people often suffer from back pain when sitting all day long, rounding the back when looking at our phone, etc. - so it’s definitely time to strengthen this part of our body & show it some love (and a beautiful burn haha).
