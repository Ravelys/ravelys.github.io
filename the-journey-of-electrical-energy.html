An educational short animation film explaining the production of electricity in Western Macedonia, Greece. The target audience are children attending the 5th and 6th grade of elementary School.

Coordination - Scenario- Scientific Supervision: Anna Spyrtou - Assistant Professor (Department of Primary Education)
