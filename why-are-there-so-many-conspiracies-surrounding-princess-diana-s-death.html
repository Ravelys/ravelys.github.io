The 1997 death of Princess Diana was such an earth-shattering event for the United Kingdom and the world that people are still talking about it nearly 25 years later. While the car crash that killed Diana and boyfriend Dodi Fayed was officially ruled an accident, many observers, including some very close to Diana and Fayed, believe otherwise.

Theories about that fateful night in late August include that Diana’s chauffeur was working for the British Secret Intelligence Service, and that a member of the royal family helped orchestrate the crash. And even though officials have taken steps to disprove them, the theories persist. It raises the question: Why are there so many conspiracies surrounding Princess Diana’s death?
