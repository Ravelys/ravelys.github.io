We guarantee you that this talk is worth at least a thousand words. And much more.
Vilas Nayak, an acclaimed speed painter of India’s got talent fame, is one of the most sought after artists in the world. His performances on Asia’s Got Talent on AXN and Got Talent World Stage Live have caught the attention of millions. He has received recognition from respected figures like Sachin Tendulkar, Abdul Kalam and even international celebrities like Pele and David Foster! His live artwork talks about various social issues and aims to bring harmony and peace in society.
In this talk, Vilas discusses how he went from a dissatisfied HR Talent Manager to a world renown painter by simply not letting fear cloud his vision and encourages everyone to find an unique niche to excel in. And, worry not, talking is not all he does.
Watch Vilas Nayak’s astounding performance as he creates two masterpieces in the blink of an eye! Vilas Nayak is a self-taught speed painter from India who started painting at the age of 3. He shot to fame by being a finalist of India’s Got Talent, and went on to participate in Asia’s Got Talent and, subsequently, the Grand Talent world stage. He has performed in over 23 countries all over the world and was the winner of the ‘EC live quotient Most Innovative Act Award’ in 2012. He has also performed in front of the United Nations General Assembly and during the NBA halftime show.
