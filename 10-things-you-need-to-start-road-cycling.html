Cycling can seem as simple as getting dressed and getting on your bike, but there are a few things you’ll need before you’re prepared set off out on the road. Spare tubes, mini pumps, and multi-tools are all vital if you’re setting out on a solo ride, and you need to keep yourself fuelled too.

Watch Manon, Hank and Ollie take us through a few things you’ll want to remember to take with you out on the road to make sure you and your bike make it to the end of your ride!
