Authorities say there is a very high chance that Brian Laundrie was dead only days after Gabby Petitio was reported missing. Last month, investigators reportedly mistook Brian’s mother, Roberta Laundrie, for her son. Officials had said they were aware of Brian’s whereabouts during a press conference on September 16. Earlier this week, Taylor told WINK News that Brian and his mother have a similar build.
