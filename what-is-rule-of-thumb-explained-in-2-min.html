In this video, we will explore What is Rule of Thumb
We will look at what it is, how does it work, why it’s important and I will also share some examples to illustrate it.

Rule Of Thumb refers to a principle with broad application that is not intended to be strictly accurate or reliable for every situation.
In simple words, the Rule Of Thumb is a rough estimation based on person previous experience. Or it can be treated as the most common way of doing things.
If you asked me what time you should wake up if you want to start the day early then I would say: ”As a rule of thumb people who wake up early start their day at 5 am”
Rule Of Thumb is a common knowledge that people practice, but it’s not set in stone and the answer can vary.
The term Rule Of Thumb is widely used and people accept it as a rough estimate when it’s given.
It’s important to remember that the Rule Of Thumb might be quite inaccurate so you have to take it with all the responsibility.
And you can’t keep the person accountable if the answer turned to be out completely inaccurate. Here are few more examples of Rule Of Thumb:
-) As a Rule Of Thumb, most people are more productive in the mornings.
-) A good Rule Of Thumb you should exercise at least 30 minutes 4-5 times a week.
-) Going by the Rule Of Thumb you can have one cheat meal per week
-) As a Rule Of Thumb, you should include 1-hour buffer time every day.

I hope that you enjoyed this video and I was able to give you a quick summary of what Rule of Thumb is and how you can use it in your everyday life.
