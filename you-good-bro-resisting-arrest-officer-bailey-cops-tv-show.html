Officer Bailey of the Wichita Police Department tracks down a red chevy sonic. When Officer Bailey pulls over the vehicle, he informs the suspects of his name and the reason why they were asked to pull over was that their tint tags were fake. The suspects are asked to step out of the car, this is when the passenger begins to struggle and resist being searched. During the search, the suspect drops a bag of illegal drugs and resists arrest. He continued to claim he didn’t do anything. The officers have no other choice but to take down the suspect as he resists arrest. Meanwhile, Officer Bailey arrests the driver for carrying an unlicensed weapon.
