AMAZING DIY DECORATIONS TO MAKE YOUR HOME LOOK MODERN



Today, I show you some amazing tricks on how to make up with amazing home decorations and make your apartment sparkle. So, let’s get creative and reuse old stuff.

You can use the lid from a box to create adorable all decorations. You can even give them different colors, such as light grey, dark grey and medium shade grey to make things interesting.

Another great idea is to take an old pumpkin, pour cement over it and create an amazing bowl or even sink with it. To make it appear more realistic for the holidays, I show you how to paint it to make it look like a real pumpkin.

I also show you some great ideas on how to make your own plant pots by mixing cement and some polystyrene bubbles. Then, you use a plastic bucket to get the shape of your planet pot, let it dry and voila. As a bonus, you can add some paint on it to make it appear more realistic.

You can do something similar with broken baskets. You pour some cement over them, shape them with a sharp object so that it looks like they have little rocks on them and then paint them all sorts of colors

Watch until the end to find out how to use white paint, gold paint, and cement to an adorable lamp for your coffee table and many more.
