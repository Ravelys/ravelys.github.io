The star i discuss has 5 points:
Inclusion, communication, collaboration, sense-based learning and place-based learning. In the middle it says attention, as the morning activities conducted within school are all based around ‘attention’.
This glow is inspired by Deleuze & Guattari (1988) work, using the ‘and.. and.. and..’ concept, that this glow is constantly moving with an open and flexible environment and that is why I feel you can use it within every lesson, every day. As attention is empirical for learning and we should therefore foster this!
