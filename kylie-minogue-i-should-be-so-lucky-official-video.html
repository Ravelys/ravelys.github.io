“I Should Be So Lucky” was Kylie’s debut single in the UK (her 2nd in Australia) and a global smash reaching the number 1 spot in the UK (for 5 weeks), Australia (6 weeks), Germany and Japan. The video was filmed in Melbourne at the studios of Channel 7. The song was recorded in less than an hour and written and recorded by the production team of Stock Aitken and Waterman. It features on her 7 x UK platinum debut “Kylie” and also appears on the soundtrack for the Emily Lloyd/Peter Falk 1989 comedy “Cookie”.
