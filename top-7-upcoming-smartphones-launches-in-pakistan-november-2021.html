In this video, We are going to talk about upcoming smartphone launches in Pakistan in November 2021, The upcoming smartphones are Vivo Y21, ZTE mobiles like Blade A51, A31 & Blade V51, realme Narzo 50i or 50a & realme GT Master Edition, Poco M4 Pro, Tecno Camon 18, Tecno Camon 18 Pro & Tecno Camon 18 Premiere and Infinix Note 11 series

Infinix Note 11 series phone is coming with an updated design, the design includes a refreshed rear panel and the main camera reveals a 64MP ultra night camera setup with 30x telescope zoom and 120Hz IPS LCD panel with 1,080 x 2,460 px resolution. It features Helio G96 chipset, GPU is G57 MC2

Xiaomi Poco lineup has a new phone coming up soon, Poco M4 Pro 5G will be a follow-up of the M3 Pro 5G and Poco M4 comes with 6.5’’ IPS LCD 1080 x 2400 Pixels & 90Hz refresh rate & triple camera on the rear side 48 MP + 5 MP + 2 MP, LED Flash & 8MP front side, M4 Pro has 2.2GHz octa-core processor with Mediatek Dimensity 700 and 5000mAh battery & 18W fast charging

Realme GT Master consists of a triple-camera 64 MP + 8 MP + 2 MP while on the front there is a single camera, 32 MP (wide). The sensors include Fingerprint (under display, optical), accelerometer, gyro, proximity, and compass, Realme GT master edition comes in 8GB/12GB of RAM and 128/256 built-in storage, The phone is powered by a MediaTek dimensity 700 & 2.4 Octa-core processor, GPU is Adreno 642L. The smartphone provides 1080 x 2400 pixels FHD+ display and the screen size of the device is 6.43’’ Super AMOLED with 120Hz refresh rate & 360Hz touch sampling, The smartphone is fueled with a non-removable Li-Po 4300 mAh + 65 Fast charging

Tecno Camon 18 Series roundup series comprises the standard Camon 18 paired with the Camon 18 Premier with Gimbal camera in action

Tecno Camon 18, 18P smartphones have silently launched in Nigeria, both phones fitted 6.8’’ punch-hole display, camon 18 have a 90Hz refresh rate & 18P comes with a 120Hz FHD+ display with a resolution of 1080 x 2460pixels. The Camon 18 & 18P have 16MP selfie camera and on the rear side camon 18 have 48MP+2+2 and camon 18P have 48MP+2+13MP portrait lens

Camon 18 comes with Helio G88 chipset with 4GB/128GB while camon 18P gets the Helio G96 chip with 8GB/128GB, Both phones have 5000mAh battery, camon 18 supports 18W fast charging & 18P supports 33W charging
