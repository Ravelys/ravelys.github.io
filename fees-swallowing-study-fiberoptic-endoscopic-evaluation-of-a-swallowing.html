A FEES swallowing study is a test that is done in a clinic setting. During a FEES swallowing study, a speech pathologist works with the ENT Pediatric Surgeon to use a special camera to look in the back of the nose and throat to watch patients swallow and assure that muscles in the throat are working properly.
