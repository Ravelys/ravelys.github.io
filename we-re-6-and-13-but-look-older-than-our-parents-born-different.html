SIBLINGS Anjali, 13, and Keshav, 6, were both born with a rare condition called ‘Cutis Laxa’ that makes them appear much older than their age. A form of progeria, it causes their skin to lose elasticity. Older sister Anjali was diagnosed at just one month old, after her parents noticed her skin was unusually dry. Unfortunately for the family, younger brother Keshav also started showing symptoms at an early age. Although the two siblings’ lives aren’t majorly affected by the condition, the parents are keen to discover a cure or treatment. Anjali and Keshav are inseparable and support each other through difficulties such as people staring when they are out and about. Their father Shatrugan told Truly: "I feel proud to see my kids helping each other out."
