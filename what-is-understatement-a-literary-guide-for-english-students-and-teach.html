What does the literary term "understatement" mean? What are some examples of understatement? Professor Lily Sheehan answers these questions using Oscar Wilde’s play The Importance of Being Earnest. The short video is designed to help high school and college English students to not only identify understatement but also to analyze its structure and purpose.
