"On My Own" by Julia Pietrucha from "Parsley" the album is about realizing the borders in your life are there because you’ve put them there. Sometimes all it takes to break down ones borders is facing your fears, in my case buying a motorcycle and setting off on and eye opening adventure. This song was recorded due to the confidence gained on this journey. I hope you enjoy it -Julia

info: Julia rides a motorcycle from Hanoi, Vietnam 4,000 miles through Laos to Cambodia while writing melodies and lyrics on her ukulele. This montage is set to one of those songs, "On My Own" from her debut album - Parsley (Availible now).
