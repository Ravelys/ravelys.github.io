Hey everyone, today we’re going to be reworking five killer perks that have been somewhat forgotten, largely due to low usage and questionable power. The idea of this is to suggest changes that’ll get the perks usage up, but not necessarily make it rival the best perks in the game, just up it to a good standard whilst also making it a bit more fun. If you’ve got a good idea for any of these perks and how they could be changed, please do drop it down below. Let’s get into it.
