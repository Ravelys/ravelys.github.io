But just because we all know how to use this kitchen staple in tons of different ways, that doesn’t mean that there aren’t new, creative recipes out there to discover. In fact, these 4 bread-based recipes are about to transform the most basic of basics into spectacular snacks! From homemade pizza bites to mac & cheese toast, these tasty snacks are reinventing sliced bread. 1. Pizza Cups

Yield: 6
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You’ll Need:

- 6 slices of bread
- 1 tbsp melted garlic herb butter
- ½ cup pizza sauce
- 6–8 mozzarella pearls 
- mini pepperoni slices
- fresh basil to garnish

Prep Time: 30 minutes

- 6 hot dogs
