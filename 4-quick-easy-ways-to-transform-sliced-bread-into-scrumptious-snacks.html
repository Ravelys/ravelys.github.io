4 Unbelievable Bread-Based Snacks ⬇️ SCRUMDIDDLYUMPTIOUS RECIPES BELOW ⬇️ Covered with butter or jelly for breakfast, topped with ham and cheese for lunch, served as a side with a delicious pasta dinner, or even eaten with some dip as a quick snack for when you’re on-the-go – most of us eat bread every single day. But just because we all know how to use this kitchen staple in tons of different ways, that doesn’t mean that there aren’t new, creative recipes out there to discover. In fact, these 4 bread-based recipes are about to transform the most basic of basics into spectacular snacks! From homemade pizza bites to mac & cheese toast, these tasty snacks are reinventing sliced bread. After all, just because bread is our bread and butter, that doesn’t mean it has to be served that way!

1. Pizza Cups

Yield: 6
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You’ll Need:

- 6 slices of bread
- 1 tbsp melted garlic herb butter
- ½ cup pizza sauce
- 6–8 mozzarella pearls 
- mini pepperoni slices
- fresh basil to garnish

Yield: 3
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cooking Time: 5–10 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You’ll Need: 

- butter for frying
- 3 slices of bread

3. Hot Dog Mozzarella Sticks

Yield: 6
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You’ll Need:

- 6 slices of bread
- 6 slices of mozzarella
- 6 hot dogs
- ½ tsp paprika + ½ tsp rosemary + ½ tsp garlic powder for the breading
- 2 whisked eggs for the egg wash

Yield: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 5 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You’ll Need:

- 4 slices of bread
