Hey Guys! Today were going old school with a vanilla no mods/no DLC vortex campaign with Tyrion! This means no Free-LC, either! I wanted to get a version of the game with as much missing since we only truly appreciate some things when theyre gone. Like unit variety! It should be a fun way to saw farewell to Total War: WARHAMMER II ahead of the games third instalment while revisiting the games oldest characters. An interesting challenge not being able to rely on certain units, too! Theres plenty well have to do without, but our enemies have no such limitations :P

This LP might also prove useful for folks whove only just bought the game, but to them Id recommend looking at the DLC page and also all the free content over on "Total War Access" since theres a pile of free content for this game that Im not using here.
