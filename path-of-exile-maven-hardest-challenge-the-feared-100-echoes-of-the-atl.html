1-focus on dodging when Maven uses her skill "double", mainly because each copy of shaper is going to replicate itself resulting in 4 shapers total

5-bring a build that doesn’t suffer from reflected damage, Atziri will some of your damage back to you
6-prioritize bosses in this particular order:
