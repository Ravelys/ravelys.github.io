Improve your awareness of unstressed syllables and practice weak forms in English with Uncle Geoff.

Students of English tend to give equal stress to all the syllables in a sentence, whereas native speakers only stress the most important words in a sentence.

This lesson will help you practice and produce weak forms and unstressed syllables, which will make you sound more like a native speaker.
