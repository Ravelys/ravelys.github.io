DAYTON –Changes are coming to Dayton Children’s. The healthcare provider announced Thursday that it will stop selling sugar-sweetened soda and sports drinks at all of its locations. According to the American Academy of Pediatrics, a daily 12-ounce can of soda increases the risk of obesity in children by 60 percent. The change takes effect May 1.
