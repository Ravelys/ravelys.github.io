This texture video will also go well with an art lesson where you will be discovering texture or even making texture! Watch to see examples of what the difference is between physical texture and visual texture.

Physical Texture is what you feel when you touch the surface of something. Visual Texture (also called Implied Texture) is what you make when you draw, paint, or sculpt the surface of something.

Texture is one of the elements of art. Other elements of art - like line, value, and color - can be used to make texture!
