It was the rock star way for sure! But the real highlight of the trip was seeing Steve Stevens—one of my main guitar influences—live. He’s playing a Godin here, but it’s similar to the Washburn I’ve played on the last few videos. Tico would have been singing loud with a long way to fly to the stage, because you can’t get any higher than the nosebleed seats we had…
