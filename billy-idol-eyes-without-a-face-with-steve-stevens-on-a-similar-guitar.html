Two years ago today I went to Vegas to see Billy Idol in concert. We ended up taking a helicopter into the Grand Canyon with some close friends for a picnic lunch at the bottom of the canyon. It was the rock star way for sure! But the real highlight of the trip was seeing Steve Stevens—one of my main guitar influences—live. He’s playing a Godin here, but it’s similar to the Washburn I’ve played on the last few videos. Tico would have been singing loud with a long way to fly to the stage, because you can’t get any higher than the nosebleed seats we had…
