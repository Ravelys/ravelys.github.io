It was wide range of surfers that turned up for this session at Uluwatu. There were a few of the usual pros and shredders, but it was regular surfers that made up the bulk of the lineup.

With a mid-tide for most of the session, Main Peak and Racetracks produced some nice walls and the odd barrel without getting too shallow – so there was a little something for everyone.
