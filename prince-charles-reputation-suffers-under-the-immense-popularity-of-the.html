Buckingham Palace are reportedly focused on "bolstering" the popularity of Prince Charles, ahead of his accession to the British throne, according to a royal expert.

Jonathan Sacerdoti told Express.co.uk that the Queen’s "incredible leadership" over the last seven decades had made it difficult for the future king to "step into her shoes".

At 95 years old, Queen Elizabeth II is due to celebrate her Platinum Jubilee next June, with a four-day Bank Holiday planned as part of the festivities.
