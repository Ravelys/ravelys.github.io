French jazz singer Cyrille Aimée is one of the most undeniably talented vocalists making music today. She grew up in France surrounded by gypsy music and culture, both of which play a key role in her art today.

Aimée made her long awaited debut at The Greene Space at WNYC with a band of internationally acclaimed companions, hosted by Jonathan Schwartz. She joined us for a CD release party celebrating her new Mack Avenue Records album,"Let’s Get Lost."
