Spending too much time indoors often makes us feel tired or low on energy. This is generally caused by poor air exchange. TwinFresh ventilators are a simple fix for this problem. Based on energy recovery principle the TwinFresh units will help save the heat in the cold season and drastically reduce the heating costs.

You will no longer have to break down the walls and spread the redecoration across the entire home only to lay the air ducts and make other arrangements for the proper ventilation system operation. Just install a TwinFresh Comfo RA1-50 unit onto an outer wall of the building and enjoy the occupant comfort in your home.
