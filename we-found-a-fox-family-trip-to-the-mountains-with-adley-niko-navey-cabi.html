Niko Bear wants to bring some toys with him, so we get his bag all packed and get some him some toys out of the ultimate toy room! And Baby Navey is just happy to here lol!! After the bags are packed (and Niko Bear and Adley have gone down the slide on Adley’s bedroom club house), we load up the monster truck and get ready to hit the road.

So good to see my dad!
