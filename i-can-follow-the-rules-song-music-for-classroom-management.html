Music for Classroom Management is perfect for primary teachers that are looking for a musical way to help children learn and review classroom rules and procedures. The song "Follow the Rules" reviews some very basic rules that are probably taught in most classrooms, such as following directions, listening to the teacher, being nice to everyone, and keeping your hands to yourself.
