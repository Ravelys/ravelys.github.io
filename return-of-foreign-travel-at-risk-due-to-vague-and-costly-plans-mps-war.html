The planned restart of international travel next month is in jeopardy with “vague and costly” proposals not enough to reboot the aviation and tourism sectors, a committee of MPs has warned.

The Transport Select Committee said the Government Global Travel Taskforce report gave "insufficient" detail to allow businesses and travellers to prepare for the safe resumption of international travel as planned on May 17.
