The Mezzala position has been one of the most important tactical developments in football. It’s versatile nature makes it the perfect role to have for high pressing teams, as it allows them to regain possession early and threaten the goal often. When building up, the Mezzala will always try and find his positioning between the lines, meaning he is excessively difficult to mark and to track.
In todays tactics video, we’re going to be taking a look at the mezzala position, and the main offensive and defensive duties of this role.
