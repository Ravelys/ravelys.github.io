We’re getting our house ready to sell (we’re moving) so I am finally getting around to doing a bunch of projects that I’ve put off for 9 years. One of those projects is to replace a severely dated light fixture in our kids bathroom. In this quick project I made a new light fixture to put above the mirror using a reclaimed piece of wood flooring and some basic light fixtures from the big box home center. It’s easy to customize and anyone can do it!
