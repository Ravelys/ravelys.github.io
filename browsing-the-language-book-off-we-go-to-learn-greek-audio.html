Take a quick look at the Greek book Φύγαμε για ελληνικά! , written by Omilo teachers Konstantinos, Marina and Dora. https://www.omilo.com/publications/fygame-greek-language-book/
The book is intended for adult learners of Modern Greek as a foreign/second language who are at an intermediate level.
The book is also accompanied by MP3 down-loadable audio files, which you will receive in your inbox on the order confirmation, after your payment.
As the title implies, you are invited to join us on a voyage of discovery which offers you the opportunity to practice your Greek reading and listening comprehension skills and to enrich your vocabulary.
This eBook covers a wide range of everyday life topics and it also provides information on various social and cultural aspects of life in Greece.
It is not possible to print the eBook.
If you prefer the printed version, please buy the “printed book”, which you can also find on the right of this webpage.
