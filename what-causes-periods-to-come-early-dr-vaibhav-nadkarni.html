There are multiple factors that can cause a period to come early. If periods happen every once you don’t have to worry, as variations in the menstrual cycle are common. Early periods are due to hormonal changes, mostly during puberty.

If periods come early and are predictable after the age of 35+ are normally due to premenopause.

Consult a gynecologist if you have any doubt.
