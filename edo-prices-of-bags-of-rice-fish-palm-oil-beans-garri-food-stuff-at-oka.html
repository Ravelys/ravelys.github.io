In today’s update, our focus is directed to the current prices of food items and our journey was at Oka Market at Upper Sokponba Road In Benin City.

During our visit to the market, it was observed that the sellers who sells bags of rice at wholesale prices lamented on the current price.

According to one of the sellers, A bags of rice’s goes from between 25,000naira to 35,000naira depending on the make.

We also visited a shop where different food items are being sold and the owner of the shop further gave extra explanation as to why the prices are such on a higher side.
