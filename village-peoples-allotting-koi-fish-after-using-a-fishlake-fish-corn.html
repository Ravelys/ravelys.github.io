Friend; this is basically the village fish distribution process by the village Fish Distributor after catching lots of koi fish & Bangladeshi Fish, especially Golden Koi, black koi fish & green koi. Basically the fishlake was full of Bangladeshi Fish so, within very short period of time; fishers managed enough koi and murrel; of course it was a Long Fishlake. And fishermans village used their fishing net as the main trading equipment.

I really enjoyed the scenario of lots of Koi fish distribution process & number of fishes were live koi fish. Even, I was so much surprised to see the incredible number: how much are koi fish. Lots of koi fish; friends!

Yes, of course it was cheap koi fish because of the heavy number and Fish Distributor done their job (allotting) very smartly.
