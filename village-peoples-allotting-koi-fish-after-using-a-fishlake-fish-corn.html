Friend; this is basically the village fish distribution process by the village Fish Distributor after catching lots of koi fish & Bangladeshi Fish, especially Golden Koi, black koi fish & green koi. On the other hand, village peoples also managed enough numbers of Snakehead Murrel. After using the fresh koi fish Fish Distributor used village Murrel.
Basically the fishlake was full of Bangladeshi Fish so, within very short period of time; fishers managed enough koi and murrel; of course it was a Long Fishlake. And fishermans village used their fishing net as the main trading equipment.

I really enjoyed the scenario of lots of Koi fish distribution process & number of fishes were live koi fish. Even, I was so much surprised to see the incredible number: how much are koi fish. Lots of koi fish; friends!

Yes, of course it was cheap koi fish because of the heavy number and Fish Distributor done their job (allotting) very smartly. Basically, in this season I don’t need to take extra time on think of: where to get koi fish? Berceuse, Bangladeshi koi is available here and there in all of the fishlake. So, koi fish near me in this season. By the way, in this fish distribution process, sometimes village people’s allotted as a koi fish signally to merge the distribution process.
