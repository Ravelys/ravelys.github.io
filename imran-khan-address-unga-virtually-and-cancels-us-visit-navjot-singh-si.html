The high-level segment of the 76th Session of UNGA will be held in New York from 21-27 September 2021.
During his address at Shanghai Cooperation Organization (SCO) summit (September 17-18) in Tajikistan, PM Khan called upon regional countries to chart out “a coordinated approach towards stabilizing the situation in Afghanistan” by providing humanitarian support.
