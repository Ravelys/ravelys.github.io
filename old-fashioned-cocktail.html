My ALL TIME favorite drink is an old fashioned cocktail. It’s the alcoholic equivalent to pizza! When it’s done right it’s amazing! When it’s done poorly, it’s not all that bad, either. I however have no patience for a mediocre cocktail so you know this wil be done the RIGHT way! A flamed orange peel, bitters on a sugar cube, bourbon and as a personal touch, a very nice cherry to add a bit of sweetness to the delicious drink.

Full Recipe: https://preppykitchen.com/old-fashioned/

Is it strong? Very! But sometimes a cocktail needs to be!! What’s your favorite bourbon? Always love to try new brands out.
