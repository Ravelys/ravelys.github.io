Buddy Guy and John Mayer perform "What Kind of Woman Is This?" live at Farm Aid 2005 at the First Midwest Bank Amphitheater in Tinley Park, Illinois on September 18, 2005.

For more information about Farm Aid, visit: http://farmaid.org/youtube
