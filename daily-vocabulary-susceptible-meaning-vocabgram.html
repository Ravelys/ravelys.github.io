Daily Vocabulary: In this lesson, I have explained the word "Susceptible” with examples. Learn this English vocabulary word and use it in your daily conversation. It will help in improving your English Fluency.

Meaning:
Easily influenced or affected by something

Sentences:
1. Girls are susceptible to flattery.
2. Poverty makes a person susceptible to corruption.
3. People with a weak immune system are susceptible to diseases.
