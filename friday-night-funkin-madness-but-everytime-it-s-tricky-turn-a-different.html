This bonus weeks song is called Madness and it comes from the Vs. Tricky Version 2 or The Full-Ass Tricky Mod. In the past I did a madness but a different skin mod is used video so now I wanted to do a full madness cover. I have to admit that while recording I had less trouble with the first half compared to second half which lead to notes flying or other issues. I looked at various other madness cover videos to gain a better look at how I wanted to format the majority of each mod. Once again I still want people to know that I did use a bot to keep things well sync because I don’t intend on hiding that fact since I wanted to make this video as a fun viewing experience while delivering people exposure of new skin mods out there. Overall, madness will stand by to be the greatest and most memorable Friday Night Funkin’ mod song. I am truly proud to have made another video on it. Hope you enjoy the video!
