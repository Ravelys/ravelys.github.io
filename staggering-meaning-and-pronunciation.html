IPA Transcription of staggering is /stˈægɚ.ɪŋ/.

Definition of staggering according to Wiktionary:
staggering can be a verb, an adjective or a noun

As a verb staggering can mean:
The participle form of stagger.
As an adjective staggering can mean:
Incredible, overwhelming, amazing.
As a noun staggering can mean:
1. The motion of one who staggers.
2. That which staggers something or somebody.
3. In animation, the repetition of a sequence of frames to show struggling effort.
