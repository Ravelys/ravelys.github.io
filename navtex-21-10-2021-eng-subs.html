BREAKING NEWS.
Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS. FRANCE. USA. ISRAEL. EGYPT.
According to the Greek news website Proto Thema, Cyprus has released a new NAVTEX for the Nautical Geo research vessel.
More specifically, this new NAVTEX concerns an area south of Cyprus, which we see in the graph we created, through the Google Earth application.
The dates mentioned are from October 21, ie today, until 23, while at this time, at 13:00 in Greece, according to the online platform marinetraffic, the vessel remains moored off the port of Larnaca.
Of course, this will be a first test for the Cypriot government to see if the Turks will harass the ship again, as they had done a while ago west of Paphos.
A test not for the faint of heart, we would say, as Greece and Cyprus are not prepared to succumb to the threats and to the illegal actions of Turkey.
