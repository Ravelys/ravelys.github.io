BREAKING NEWS.
GREAT BRITAIN. USA.
According to Greek and foreign media, a British F-35 fighter jet crashed a few hours ago in the Mediterranean Sea.
More specifically, the state-of-the-art fighter belonged to the strike force of the aircraft carrier Queen Elisabeth and had taken off to perform normal routine flights, when for some unknown reason, the pilot was forced to ejected and finally be rescued and returned safely to the aircraft carrier.
This was announced by the press office of the British Ministry of Defense, adding that an investigation has already been launched into the causes of the crash and therefore it will not make any further comment.
Let us simply mention that British and US F-35 fighter jets are on board the Queen Elisabeth aircraft carrier and that this particular ship is leading a fleet of ships, a naval strike force that recently operated in Iraq and Syria.
