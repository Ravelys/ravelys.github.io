Structure:
…………..
POSITIVE SENTENCE
……………………………….
[Sub + will have to + v1 + ob]
Ex: You will have to go to market.
That girl will have to marry me.
……………………………………………………………………………………………..
NEGATIVE SENTENCE
………………………………..
[Sub + will not have to + v1 + ob]
Ex: You will not have to do anything.

………………………………………………………………………………………………..
INTERROGATIVE SENTENCE
……………………………………….
[Will + sub + have to + v1 + ob]
Ex: Will you have to do this work?
……………………………………………………………………………………………….
INTERROGATIVE + NEGATIVE
……………………………………………
[Won’t + sub + have to + v1 + ob]
Ex: Won’t you have to go there?
