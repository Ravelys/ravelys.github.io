A theoretical "genocide route" battle theme for Noelle from Deltarune, i tried using different songs from the OST that have to do with Noelle and incorporate them into this song

I tried to aim for a mix of "Deltarune style" and my own style, but of course i am not Toby Fox, so it cannot sound exactly like him, nevertheless, i tried my best to make it feel close to something that could actually be in the game
