The eight sign is inviting you to group dates. If she does this, just reschedule.

The ninth sign is having things to do after the date. If she says she’s going to do something after, just reschedule. If she has something else to do, don’t even go.

The tenth sign is cancelling last minute. This is the biggest sign of disinterest. The next time she wants to hang out, be difficult.
