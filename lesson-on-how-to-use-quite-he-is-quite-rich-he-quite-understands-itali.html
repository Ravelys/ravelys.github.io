Quite is an adverb and so it modifies adverbs and adjectives. However, #quite can have many meanings in different contexts.
Mr. P./Marc will explain all these meanings by giving a diagram with different easy examples for students to understand.
This lesson is designed for elementary students but even for advanced students of English who wish to improve their grammar.
