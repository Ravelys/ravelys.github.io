Choices have consequences! This statement is easier to say for those who have many choices to pick from. Growing up in Jericho estate, the consequences of a wrong turn were often life-ending. Antony Ndung’u (Tosh) tell of living in the hood, watching his friends making wrong decisions, being rejected by football and finding a lifeline in theatre and acting. Tosh; an award-winning actor and producer story shows that patience and doing the right things right eventually pays. His greatest achievement is that he has made his mama proud.
