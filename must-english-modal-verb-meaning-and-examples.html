The meaning and different uses of the English modal verb MUST.
We look at how to write sentences using MUST correctly.
We see different situations where MUST is used including:
- Obligation, Duty
- To emphasize the necessity of something
- Deduction - when you are certain about something
- Logical Assumptions using Must + Have + Past Participle
- Strong Recommendations and Advice

We look at the negative of Must … Mustn’t
See look at its negative meaning with example sentences.

We end with a simple exercise to practice MUST and MUSTN’T
Includes a summary chart.
