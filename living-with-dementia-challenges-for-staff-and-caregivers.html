This site provides a mini-lecture series related to the art and science of public health by Dr. Wally J. Bartfay. In this lecture, we discuss various clinical challenges facing patients with dementia. Perspectives and insights from various front line clinical staff and experts from Ontario Shores Centre for Mental Health Sciences in Whitby, Ontario Canada are provided.
