Snorkeling For Beginners		 Quick Tips on How to Snorkel
Getting Started:
1. Get a snorkel & mask
2. Put on mask correctly
3. Lay flat on water
4. Bite on mouth piece
5. Breath slowly
6. Wear buoyancy vest

Keep Your Airway Clear
1. Breath carefully
2. Try hold your breath
3. Surface your head
4. Practice Exhale
5. Expel remaining water
6. Master airway control
7. Practice diving

Swimming with Snorkel
1. Use Fins on your feet
2. Hold your arms at your side
3. Kick slowly and powerfully
4. Kick farther down
5. Keep fins below water
6. Try floating the waves
7. Try to conserve energy

Good Snorkel Experience
1. Choose a good spot
2. Choose a sunny day to snorkel
3. Learn different fish and coral
