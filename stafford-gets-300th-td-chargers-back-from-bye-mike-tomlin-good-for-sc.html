On the latest episode of the LA Football Podcast, Ryan gets into all the LA Football storylines that happened over the weekend.

UCLA fought back against Oregon but came up just a bit short. Is Chip Kelly on the hot seat?

Finally, the Chargers are back from their bye week. Are they ready for redemption?
