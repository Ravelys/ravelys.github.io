On the latest episode of the LA Football Podcast, Ryan gets into all the LA Football storylines that happened over the weekend.

The Rams got the win, but should there be concern over how close it was? Then on Monday, the Rams traded starting linebacker Kenny Young for a future draft pick. Why?

UCLA fought back against Oregon but came up just a bit short. Is Chip Kelly on the hot seat?

USC lost to rival Notre Dame in a game that was never really close. Another stain on a lost year, but a new coach’s name was thrown into the ring. Reason for excitement?

Finally, the Chargers are back from their bye week. Are they ready for redemption?
