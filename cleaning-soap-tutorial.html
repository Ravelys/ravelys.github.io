This video will go over the basics of home soapmaking, and then show you how to make a beginner soap recipe.

This bar is 100% coconut oil and is used for household cleaning. It’s a great soap bar for laundry stains and household stains and can be used with a brush to wash dishes. You can grate it into flakes and mix with borax to make an a scouring powder. You can hand wash clothing with it when you travel. It is very versatile, but not suitable for skin.
