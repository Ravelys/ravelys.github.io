Home Is by Justin Vernon, released under the name J.D. Vernon in 2001.
He was twenty years old when he released the album, and had written the songs from 1998 to 2001. It contains duets of his sister Kim Vernon (Feel The Light & Morning a.m.), his brother Nate Vernon (Morning a.m.) and Sara Emma Jensen (Leave It Alone Blues). This album was released six years prior to Justin Vernon’s creation of Bon Iver. This is his first solo album, and very few copies of it were released.
