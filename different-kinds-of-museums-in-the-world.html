War museum/ military museum showcasing military artifacts, artillery, photographs, uniforms, etc., A mobile museum that goes from place to place. Torture museum displays torture devices. A House museum is usually a museum made famous by a certain person or happening. All these museums are featured in this video together with all their definitions.
