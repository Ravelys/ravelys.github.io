A WOMAN with a rare condition is following her dream to become a professional model. India, 24, from Atlanta, USA was born with Treacher Collins syndrome - a rare genetic disorder characterised by distinctive abnormalities of the head and face. Since she was young, India has always wanted to be a model - and represent women with a unique appearance. She said: “I’ve always wanted to be a model. I’m not what other people say I am, I am what I believe I am."
