In the third episode of LIVE ART CHAT, we are back to Buxton, Derbyshire UK and live from Lin’s new studio in her home. In this episode, Lin talks about moving into her new space and in the upheaval of moving, finding an old painting that she has decided to work on. In the demonstration, Lin shows us how she approaches reworking this older piece.
