Melfyn the Magic Scout is on an extraordinary quest to reach Mount Moral and win the trophy! On her journey, she will encounter tricky situations that will test her ability to make the right choice.

They will learn about how their choices impact others as well as themselves, and may even discover that in the end it’s not winning a prize that counts, but how they feel inside when they do the right thing…
