As the search for Brian Laundrie continues at the Carlton Reserve, law enforcement is using various methods to locate him.

That includes K-9s, according to the Pasco County Sheriff’s Office — an agency located north of the North Port and Venice area.

The agency says two K-9 units are assisting the North Port Police Department, the Sarasota County Sheriff’s Office and the FBI’s search. The police K-9s, the Pasco County Sheriff’s Office added, have assisted in the search several times before. https://bit.ly/3p2wFIh
