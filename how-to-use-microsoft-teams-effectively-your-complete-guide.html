This is the COMPLETE GUIDE to Microsoft Teams! It’s a step-by-step guide to Microsoft Teams for beginners. You’ll learn how to use teams to collaborate, manage chats, meetings and files in one place.

- Teams view and channel organization

- How to use Wiki in Teams (with example)

- How to schedule a meeting in Teams
- Calendar view in Teams
- Files View
- Chat in Teams
- How to hide and mute chat / how to unhide and unmute chat
- Commands in Teams / shortcuts
- Bookmark or save messages so you find them easier
- Notifications in Teams / getting notified when a team member becomes available
- Changing your status in teams
