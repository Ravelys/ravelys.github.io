About Clay Mixer:
Tiny, the character who was named due to his significantly small figure. His idealistic shape was born from a real clay mixer. Even though having a poor and rusted personal background, he finds for himself the sense of humor with the help from many other clay friends to get through daily silly struggles. With the help from them, Tiny will get through daily silly moments. 6 days per week, Tiny appears again to tell the audience the stories.
All episodes are made under the use of stop motion animation technique and owned by SCONNECT CO., LTD., audiojungle.net and licenced under Creative Commons Attribution.
