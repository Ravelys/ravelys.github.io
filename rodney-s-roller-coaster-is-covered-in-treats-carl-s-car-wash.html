Rodney will have to roll his coaster on over to Carl’s Car Wash! With a quick mess assessment and a few twists of some knobs, Carl’s suds-sational car wash is ready to give Rodney’s vehicle a good scrub-a-dub! Rodney leaves one happy customer, and is ready to head back to the carnival!
