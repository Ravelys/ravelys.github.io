While no yoga postures should be treated as prescriptions (they should all be incorporated into a more complete yoga practice), these 6 postures, plus sun salutations, are considered to give the most benefits. Please remember that you benefit from all yoga postures, at the level at which you are. Enjoy! Demonstrated by Ruth and Mahashakti. Camera: Eduard. Speaker: Sita.
