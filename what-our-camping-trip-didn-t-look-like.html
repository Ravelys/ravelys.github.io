We are roughing it on our impromptu journey up the Western Australian coast. Coffee and fish out of the boot with one burner between 5. But it’s pretty great just hanging out on the beach and making do. We’re around some great people and some great water. Seriously great water. Up here is teeming with so much life and great dive spots. If you get the chance to come to these parts of WA you honestly won’t regret it. It’s so worth how long it takes here, it rivals the best of what we’ve seen in the world and its remoteness is actually a blessing.

Also! Stick around for some real talk at the end, we want to share some thoughts with you guys on how we’re travelling.
