We were literally speechless after riding the S-Works Turbo Creo SL and Specialized Turbo Creo Comp Carbon crafted by geniuses that developed the Tarmac, Venge and Diverge — #specialized. We can tell that they have all the needs (and even wants) of different riders in mind while creating these rad rides and boy they are tough to top!

Here’s is our initial review and we can’t wait to hit the climbs and take them with us in an unforgettable adventure.

We’d like to thank Kalif of Specialized Malaysia for reaching out to us and for making this dream collaboration happen and of course to Ritchie of Specialized PH for sharing what these bikes have to offer with much gusto and for personally handing these bikes over to us!
