This video corrects the common misconceptions of what a quantity surveyor is. A student takes a peek into the work-life of a Quantity Surveyor (QS), as we see what a quantity surveyor does in-office and on-site. The QS featured in the video has a candid definition of her profession as a 1/3 accountant; 1/3 lawyer; and 1/3 engineer. She shares her passion in her profession, demonstrates the industry’s advancement in technology with the use of Building Information Modelling (BIM), and clarifies the student’s queries on the roles and responsibilities of a Quantity Surveyor in a simple and comprehensive manner.
