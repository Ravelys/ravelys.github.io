What’s the difference between further and farther? How do you know when to use "further" or farther" in your sentence? It’s actually very easy, and that’s what you’ll learn in this video!

Most people, including native English speakers, are confused by "further" and "farther" and they’re not sure which one to use. Many even think that they’re interchangeable and it doesn’t matter which one you use.

There’s an easy trick to use to remember if you need "further" or "farther" in your sentence.

Use "farther" for physical distance and use "further" for metaphorical/figurative distance. If you’re ever in doubt and you’re not sure which one to use, use "further".

In this video, we’ll review these rules in detail and we’ll review example sentences so you can compare "further" and "farther" in a natural context.
