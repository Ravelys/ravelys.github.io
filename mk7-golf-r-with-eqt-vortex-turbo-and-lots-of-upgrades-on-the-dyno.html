It’s been a minute since we’ve done a turbo upgrade on an MK7R. It’s very impressive what’s capable out of these stock framed IS20/IS38 turbo’s from EQT. This is the EQT Vortex Standard version. With lots of upgrades, it’s time to put it on the rollers and see what it can do on our 91oct fuel.
