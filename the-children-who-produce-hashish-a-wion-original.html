Malana is a picturesque town in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, it is secluded and cocooned in its cultural and social fabric. However, amidst the beauty, lurks a dark secret. Malana’s economy is heavily dependent on the production of hashish, many children and teenagers are involved in this process. And as the rubbing season unfolds, everything, including their education, takes a backseat.
