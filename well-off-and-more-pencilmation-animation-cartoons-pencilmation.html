Pencilmation is a web cartoon created by Ross Bollinger in which pencil drawn stick figures and doodles come to life. The Pencilmation channel is for not-too-serious grown-ups. Follow the new, wacky, and often times quite silly adventures of Pencilmate, Little Blue Man, Pencilmiss and other doodles.
