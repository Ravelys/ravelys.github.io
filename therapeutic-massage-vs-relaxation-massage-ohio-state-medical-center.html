Michele Mack, a licensed massage therapist at The Ohio State University Wexner Medical Center, explains the differences between therapeutic massage and full-body relaxation massages. In therapeutic massage within a medical setting, the goal is to focus on where you’re experiencing pain, treating that area and figuring out what’s causing the discomfort.
