Educational video for children to learn what the environment is and how we can look after it better. These are our ten tips and advice to look after the environment: turn off the lights and the electric devices youre not using, dont use plastic bags, recycle waste, turn off the water taps, dont use the toilet as a rubbish bin, have a shower instead of a bath, dont waste water leaving the tap on while brushing your teeth, reuse paper, pick up your litter on the beach or the mountain and look after the living beings around us. Doing simple things like these, you are helping to look after the planet. Want to join us in our mission?
