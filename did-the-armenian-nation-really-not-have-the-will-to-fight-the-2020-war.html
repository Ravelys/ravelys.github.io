Georgi Derlugian, a historical sociologist, in conversation with CivilNet’s Eric Hacopian, discusses the state of Armenia’s development one year after the 2020 Nagorno-Karabakh war. Professor Derlugian speaks on the divisions in Armenian society, democratization in the country, and the issues the Armenian Armed Forces is facing.
