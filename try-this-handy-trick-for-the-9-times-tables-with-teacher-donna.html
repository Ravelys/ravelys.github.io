Teacher Donna LOVES math! Today she’s here to teach you a trick to help you become more familiar and confident with the challenging 9 times tables. Follow Teacher Donna’s simple instructions and you’ll be a master of multiplication in no time!

Familiarity and efficiency with the times tables are an essential building block in mathematics. Knowing your multiplication facts opens the door to multi-digit multiplication, demystifies the abstract processes of long division as well as simplifying fractions, and lays the foundation for learning algebra. Through knowing their times tables, students will start to notice patterns and then be able to apply their knowledge to more advanced concepts. Neat!

Share this trick with anyone who will listen and remember to practice your times tables regularly.
