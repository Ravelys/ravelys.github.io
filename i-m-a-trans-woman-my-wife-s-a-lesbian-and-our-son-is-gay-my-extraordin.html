AFTER 15 years of happy marriage as husband and wife, and with two young sons, Jenni and Sarah rolled over to each other in bed one morning, each with an admission to make. Jenni confessed she was "gay" and Sarah that she was "trangender". The couple told Truly: “We quickly went from a family no-one would look at twice to a family everyone would look at." Despite the changes, they remain happily married with a loving family, and an example of true love - although not everyone agrees online. The couple have faced negativity but refuse to let that hold them back from being visible and an inspiration to others. Sarah recently underwent full gender confirmation surgery and has been documenting her journey on social media. Today is a big day as she will be wearing a swimsuit for the first time post surgery - and it feels like a chapter of her journey is now closed.
