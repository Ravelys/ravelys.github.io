I cannot tell you how many people I know who say they want to learn how to foam roll, buy one and never use it - seriously, it’s just collecting dust. * Improved recovery
* Better flexibility & mobility
* Decrease risk of injury
* Great for warm-up and cool down
* Decrease muscle soreness
