Want to learn how to foam roll? Then grab your foam roller and do it with me! It’s time to put this amazing piece of equipment for recovery to good use. I cannot tell you how many people I know who say they want to learn how to foam roll, buy one and never use it - seriously, it’s just collecting dust. * Improved recovery
* Better flexibility & mobility
* Decrease risk of injury
* Great for warm-up and cool down
* Decrease muscle soreness
