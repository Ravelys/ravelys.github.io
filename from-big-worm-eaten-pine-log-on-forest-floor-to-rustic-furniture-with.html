What would we do without chainsaw mills?! I had a half of a good-sized white pine log laying next to my tent for the last year that needed a good use. Do people make tables from pine? I guess I do, especially if they have so many knots and nice character. So I dragged the log from the trees with the atv and planked it up with the chainsaw mill to see what I could create. Let’s hope she likes it!
