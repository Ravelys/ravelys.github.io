TLABUNG KUM 150-NA SESQUICENTENNIAL THEME SONG (1871 - 2021)

"British sorkarin Mizo Lal te tudai tur leh an kut atanga Mary Winchester (Zoluti) la chhuak tura a rawn tirh Lt. Col. Thomas Herbert Lewin Mizoten Thangliana tia an koh chuan Tlabung khua hi kum 1871 November ni 9 khan a din a, hetih hunlai hi Mizoram chu thim chhah takin a tuam hunlai niin inrun leh mi lulak, ral leh sahrâng hmaa Pasalthate khawvela kan innghah lai hun kha a ni.

Chuti ang khawvela kan la awm lai chuan Lt. Col. TH Lewin hian Tlabung khua chu mu leh mal awmze nei tak Sawrkar administration hmanga changkanna lam hawizawnga hmasawn khua ni turin heng Post & Telegraph Office, Police Station, Inspection Bungalow te hi dinin, heng te hi Mizorama Sawrkar Office hmasa ber te an ni. Tichuan, amah TH. Lewin hian a pualin lungphun mawi tak leh thuziak monument stone pawh tangka dartuiluan hmanga siamin Tlabungah hian a phun a ni."
