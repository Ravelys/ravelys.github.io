Chuti ang khawvela kan la awm lai chuan Lt. Col. Tichuan, amah TH. Lewin hian a pualin lungphun mawi tak leh thuziak monument stone pawh tangka dartuiluan hmanga siamin Tlabungah hian a phun a ni."
