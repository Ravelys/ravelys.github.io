Chris Madeley started writing children’s books sparked by sitting hours in traffic and this has since become a serious business.


She is writing children’s stories for corporate Britain to teach children about safety, ethics and corporate responsibility issues.


Next-Up supports employers with a range of services for directors, partners and employees to help them understand the impact of retirement on mental health and create a plan to use their skills and experience in new ways to ensure wellbeing.

A key part of our role is to inspire people with ideas and contacts, beyond traditional expectations.
