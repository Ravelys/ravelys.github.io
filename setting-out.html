There are three different CITB HS&E tests. The HS&E test for Operatives, Specialist test and Managers and Professionals test. Each test is made up of 50 questions. You have 45 minutes to answer all questions. They are designed to test how you respond to health and safety situations on construction sites. The Setting Out video will provide you with an overview of some of the areas covered in the test. For further support with revision, please visit: https://www.citb.co.uk/hserevision
