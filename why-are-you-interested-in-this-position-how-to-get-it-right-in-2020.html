Why are you interested in this position?
➡️ 🔶 Download my FREE Interview Questions Prep Guide here: https://link.primalcareer.com/interviewquestions

Companies use questions like this one in job interviews to assess if you’re motivated to work there, or if you’re simply looking for "any job".

In this video, I’ll help you avoid 3 common job interview mistakes you could do if you don’t prepare well, but also give you interview tips on how to answer this popular interview question properly.

3 mistakes to avoid:

- Don’t talk about benefits
- Don’t talk about long-term goals that aren’t related to this company
- Don’t Trash your past employers

Use these strategies to answer questions like “Why are you interested in this position? “, “ Why do you want to work here?” or "What Are You Looking For In This Position"

- Talk about the skills that you’ll bring to this company and how it benefits you as well
- Talk about your desired career path within this new company
- Mention how you fit with the company culture

Those were my interview tips to answer the popular interview question “Why are you interested in this position? “. Let me know if you have any questions below!
