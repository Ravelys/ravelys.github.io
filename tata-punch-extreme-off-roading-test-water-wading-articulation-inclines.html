2021 Tata Punch finally unveiled in India! All New Tata Punch is an all new segment of car, which Tata called Sub-Compact SUV. This subcompact SUV sits below the 4metre compact SUV segment. Tata motors aiming at the varied customer base for the Punch. As the market is moving towards the SUVs, Tata Motors targeted the customers from the compact sedan, compact SUVs, premium hatchbacks and compact hatchbacks. Tata Punch Price will reveal on 20 October and it will price competitively to attract all the customers of the mentioned segments. Tata Punch is built on ALFA platform same as Altroz. New Tata Punch available in 1.2L 3 Cylinder Petrol engine with Dyna Pro tech. Gearbox option includes the MT and AMT. Tata Punch Diesel is not available. Just like every other Tata, Punch is also the smashing looking design. People are going crazy on the roads when we drove it! Interior also looks and feels spacious and comfortable. 2021 Punch also loaded with features like 7 inch touchscreen system with apple carplay and android auto, auto AC, part digital cluster, cruise control, etc. Some features still missing like rear ac vents and sunroof! Tata Punch safety includes dual airbags, ABS and EBD, HSS construction, etc. Tata Punch GNCAP crash test rating will reveal before the launch on 20 Oct. Tata Punch mileage also not revealed. If Tata priced it aggressively and positioned between the Altroz and the Nexon then is going to be the winner for sure!
