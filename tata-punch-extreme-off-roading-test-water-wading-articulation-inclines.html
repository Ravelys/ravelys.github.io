Tata motors aiming at the varied customer base for the Punch. As the market is moving towards the SUVs, Tata Motors targeted the customers from the compact sedan, compact SUVs, premium hatchbacks and compact hatchbacks. Tata Punch Price will reveal on 20 October and it will price competitively to attract all the customers of the mentioned segments. Tata Punch is built on ALFA platform same as Altroz. New Tata Punch available in 1.2L 3 Cylinder Petrol engine with Dyna Pro tech. Gearbox option includes the MT and AMT. Tata Punch Diesel is not available. Just like every other Tata, Punch is also the smashing looking design. People are going crazy on the roads when we drove it! Tata Punch safety includes dual airbags, ABS and EBD, HSS construction, etc. Tata Punch GNCAP crash test rating will reveal before the launch on 20 Oct. Tata Punch mileage also not revealed.
