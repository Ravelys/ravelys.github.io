My music incorporates relaxing sounds of nature and features flute music, piano music, harp music, & violin music. I am a composer from Maine and created my channel as a place to visit, relax and find peace.

Join us as we journey through rolling green hills, majestic mountains, breathtaking fjords and the amazing diversity of New Zealand.
