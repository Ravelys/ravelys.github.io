It’s gut-wrenchingly poignant lyrics like - "What puts a hundred thousand children in the sand? Belief can… What puts a folded flag inside his mother’s hand? Belief can…" - which proves that Mayer is one of the most inspired singer/songwriters of our time, bar none.
