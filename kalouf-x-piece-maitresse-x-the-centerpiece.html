A new work in progress video watching French artist KALOUF ART executed at the "Zoo Art Show" in partnership with "La Pièce Maitresse".

"Like the piano, the panther is gentle and alluring but can be brutal and powerful. Its grace and delicacy combine with the shock of impulse, like graffiti. Under the impetus of "the centerpiece" This delicately dangerous looking piano has exchanged its lyre for an audio system whose modernism completes the old aspect in a timeless and melodious object."
