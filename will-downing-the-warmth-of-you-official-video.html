Listen to the brand new single by Will Downing entitled “ The Warmth Of You”

This signature Will Downing groove filled with love, passion, want and desire, is the tale of a man longing for his exotic significant other.

Throughout the song we join him
As he reminisces on their most intimate moments, how much she means to him, what he misses the most and the magic of their love.

As we move through the passionate thoughts we learn the steps and measures this man will take to get his precious stone back home and deep into the warmth of the one he loves.

Taken from his upcoming 25th album to be released on the Sophisticated Soul Label.
