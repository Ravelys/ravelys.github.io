Listing Description:
"Located in Saint Tropez, in the middle of a vast wooded estate of 3 hectares, 5000 m² of which are vineyards, this beautiful property built in the Mediterranean style has been totally refurbished to offer an exceptional level of performance.

Nestled in an exclusive, sought-after area, in absolute calm, the Mas du Figuier is 5 minutes from the village of Saint-Tropez, the Place des Lices, the port and the beaches of Pampelonne.

With the architecture designed to blend the elegant interior with the landscaped exteriors planted with vines, olive trees and other fruit trees, the Mas du Figuier is composed of a main residence, two guest houses and a guardian house for a total living area of over 800 sqm. The property is benefiting from three swimming pools- one 100 sqm infinity pool , a tennis court, a gym, video surveillance system, SPA, Hammam, Jacuzzi, Pétanque , an organic vegetable garden with fruit trees, a fish pond, a helicopter DZ, two garages, horse boxes…"
