When nature has a mood for creating!
An imposing and at the same time unique creature of Cretan nature is located sixty kilometers from Rethymnon on the south coast of the island.
Next to the beach of Agios Pavlos, a path leads to the beach of the dunes. A unique landscape that generously offers you its aura and tranquility …
The low clouds and the sunset have given us unique pictures to share and to be found even through the screen, near the beauty and greatness of nature …
