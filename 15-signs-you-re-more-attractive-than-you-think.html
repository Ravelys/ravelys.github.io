Do you ever wonder what makes people like you and how to know if you are attractive? Well, if you can recognize these subtle signs you’re more attractive than you think! Having said that, people’s attitude and body language toward you might tell a completely different story! They may think that you have a charming personality, and you’re easy on the eyes. They may smile at you or stare at you, or simply behave in an odd way whenever they’re around you.
