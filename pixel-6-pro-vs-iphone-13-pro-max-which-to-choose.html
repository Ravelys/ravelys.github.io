Hello all and welcome to this video comparison between the Google Pixel 6 Pro and Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max and in this video we will discuss in detail all the differences between both phones to help you decide which is going to be the better option for you. Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max features a 6.7" 120HZ Promotion OLED display, Apple A15 Bionic chip, 6GB of ram, iOS 15, and a triple rear camera with liDAR, and is capable of 4K60HDR video and adds cinematic mode.
