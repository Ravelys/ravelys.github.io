1. I am strictly pro-recovery and not pro-ana! Please heed the trigger warning if watching videos of food restriction is harmful to you! I just want to bring some awareness to what living with an eating disorder is like, and connect with other people that have similar issues, as well as using this as a diary of sorts so I can reflect on my own actions. I watch a lot of videos similar to this and they comfort me a lot so I just wanted to give it a go!

2. I’m not trying to glorify or romanticise eating disorders in any way. 4. I have ‘recovered’ from my ed before (I say that in quotes bc it clearly wasn’t a full recovery) and relapsed about a year ago. I am currently seeing a psychologist about it to try and recover again, and my family are aware of my ed, however recovery is a long and hard process that is filled with a lot of pitfalls and setbacks. Trust me, I am not idolising my disorder and as much as your comments are well intended, I don’t need 50 people telling me that this is unhealthy and I need to get help. Please take this into consideration when commenting 💕
