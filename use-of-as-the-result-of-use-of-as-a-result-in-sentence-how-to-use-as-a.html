jo ke use hota hai jab hum urdu mein kuch is tarha ke sentnces bolte hai jaise ke


Aag ki waja se woh khaufzadah thi.
Zakhamo ke nateejay mein uski mout hogayi.
Dhund ke nateejay mein parwaaz mein takheer hui.
Woh haadsay ke nateejay mein zakhmi howa hai.

Apne se dekha in sab sentences mein ke nateejay mein ya ki waja se common hai
or inhi tareko se Urdu sentences ke liye hum English mein As a result of use karte hai.
Ab chale iske kuch sentences banate hai or in sentences ko translate karte hai ta ke ap As a result ko sahi se use kar sakhe
So let’s get started

(Zakhamo ke nateejay mein uski mout hogayi.)
He died as a result of his injuries.

(Dhund ke nateejay mein parwaaz mein takheer hui.)
The flight was delayed as a result of fog.

(Woh haadsay ke nateejay mein zakhmi howa hai.)
He is injured as a result of an accident.


(Yeh selaab bahhar ki taiz baarisho ke nateejay mein aaya.)
The flood came about as a result of the heavy spring rains.

(Aag ki waja se woh khaufzadah thi.)
She was scared as a result of the fire.

200 workers have lost their jobs from this company
as a result of COVID-19.
