{% raw %}Dr Bob brings you SCP Foundation Euclid Class object, SCP-2640 Ghost Lights Animation.<br /><br />SCP 2640 is an unstable temporal anomaly centered on a 5000 m2 area near Gurdon, Arkansas. The area is a heavily forested pine thicket, bisected by a Union Pacific Railroad line that runs from northeast to southwest. The railway features one iron trestle bridge, which is the epicenter of SCP2640. No other vehicular or pedestrian roads enter the area affected by SCP-2640.
