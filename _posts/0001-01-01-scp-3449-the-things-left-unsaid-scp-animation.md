{% raw %}SCP 3449 is a Safe Class anomaly also known as The Things Left Unsaid.<br /><br />SCP-3449 refers to an anomalous spiral-bound notebook with a dark blue cover. Due to the formatting of the writing, six pages of the notbok appear to contain normal diary entries, however the words used are incoherent and often ispemlls. Other pages of the diary are either torn, or left blank.<br /><br />SCP-3449's anomalous property manifests in documentation about <br />SCP3449. All writing about SCP-3449 will contain arbitrary capitalization, misspellings; and other SYntactIcal errors.
