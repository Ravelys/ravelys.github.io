{% raw %}A Share is a portion of ownership in a company.<br /><br />In order to understand what a share is, we need to understand first what a company is.<br /><br />A Company is a legal entity that allows its owners to conduct business under a separate legal identity, limiting their personal liability.<br /><br />The original founders usually fund the newly created fictional identity with initial resources such as money and in exchange, a share of the control over the company and part of the assets in the new fictional identity are issued.<br /><br />Thus, if five founders contributed $100 for the new company in equal parts; each one will get 20% of the total share of the new company.<br /><br />Shares are usually “Issued” in financial terms.
