                                                                 My Mum's recipe for an easy, shortcut Puff Pastry is so fast to make without all the folding like a traditional pastry, but it still yields amazing results.                                                                                                 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------                                         <br />     Η συνταγή της μαμάς είναι πάρα πολύ εύκολη, μία σφολιάτα πάρα πολύ γρήγορη, για να τη φτιάξετε χωρίς όλη αυτή τη διαδικασία, που χρειάζεται η σφολιάτα, αλλά εξακολουθεί να αποδίδει εκπληκτικά αποτελέσματα.  Απολαύστε τα ζεστά. <br />       --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------                                   <br />     Cuisine : Greek  Cuisine  /   Η όλη διαδικασία θα διαρκέσει μόνο 10  - 20 λεπτά. <br />     Author : Stella Markou   /    The whole process should only take 10 - 20 minutes.<br />      Ingredients :   <br />▪3 cups (15 ½ oz / 440 g) plain flour<br />▪18 tablespoons ( 9  ½ oz/260 g) butter, frozen<br />▪2 pinches of salt<br />▪10 - 12 tablespoons chilled water<br />▪1 ½ tablespoon lemon juice                                                                                                                ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------                                                      Συστατικά :<br />▪ 3 φλιτζάνια (15 ½ oz / 440 g) απλό αλεύρι<br />▪18 κουταλιές της σούπας (9 ½ oz / 260 g) βούτυρο, κατεψυγμένο<br />▪ 2 πρέζα αλάτι<br />▪10 - 12 κουταλιές της σούπας κρύο νερό<br />▪1 ½ κουταλιά της σούπας χυμό λεμονιού                                                                                                                                -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------                                                                           Place your butter in the freezer for a minimum of 3 hours, but preferably overnight.     Frozen butter is my secret ingredient. The colder the butter the flakier the pastry. You want to add just enough water to bring the pastry together. Too much water will not yield you a flakey dough.Wrap in cling film and place in the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour. You can refrigerate it for up to 3 days or freeze to use at a later day.   Note: Once you put pastry in the fridge and it relaxes it will get a little wetter so factor that in.<br />To use your pastry: Follow instructions required by the recipe you are making. Alway bake in a HOT oven for best results.             Enjoy the hot ones.{% endraw %}
