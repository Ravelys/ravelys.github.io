{% raw %}A song to teach students the demonstratives this, that, these and those.<br /><br />Perform an action for each demonstrative pronoun. For &quot;this&quot; point to something close with one hand. For &quot;that&quot; point to something further away with one hand. For &quot;these&quot; point to several objects near you with both hands.
