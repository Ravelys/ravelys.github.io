The latest short film from Black Tongue Dakota Media (Kenn and John Little) titled Exhibited. This short film features Frank Waln, Chris Pappan, and Adam Singsinthetimber and examines how their music, art, and photography challenge stereotypes about Native people and highlights Indigenous survival in Chicago. Also featuring Starla Thompson. Thanks to Debra Yepa-Pappan for helping set up the filming at the Field Museum and Justin de Leon for his cinematography skills. Partially funded by the UMC Discipleship Ministries. Filmed at the Field Museum in Chicago, Illinois.
