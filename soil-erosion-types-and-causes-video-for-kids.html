Today we will learn about Soil Erosion.

Erosion is the process by which the surface of the Earth is worn away mainly by the action of wind, water and glacier.

There are 5 main types of soil erosion, they are:
Rraindrop or Splash erosion
Sheet erosion
Rill erosion
Gully erosion
Stream Bank erosion
