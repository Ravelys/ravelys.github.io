GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS. FRANCE. USA.
And suddenly the 110th Battle Wing in Larissa "flooded", with US F-15s, which, according to an announcement by HNDGS, came to Greece, for the exercise under the name "Castle Forge".
So while we see photos published by the US side, from the departure of the fighter jets to Greece, but also photos of the Greek HNDGS, from their arrival, to continue with the announcement, and to say that the arrival of the aircraft and the personnel support, takes place in the framework of the bilateral Military Cooperation of Greece and the USA and following actions and cooperation of US EUCOM and HNDGS.
