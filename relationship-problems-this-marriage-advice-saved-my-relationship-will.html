Relationship problems? There’s good news. These revolutionary marriage counseling techniques can save your marriage and improve every relationship in your life.

Relationship experts and married couple Harville Hendrix and Helen LaKelly Hunt are here to help. In this episode, they share the marriage counseling and relationship advice they’ve taught to thousands of couples through individual counseling and workshops. These techniques will not only lead to better, healthier relationships — they can heal the world.

Harville Hendrix and Helen LaKelly Hunt are partners in life and work. Together, they have written over 10 books of marriage advice, including three New York Times bestsellers, and the mega hit “Getting The Love You Want.” Harville and Helen co-created Imago Relationship Therapy. Harville is a couple’s therapist, educator, and clinical trainer with over 40 years experience. He’s been on The Oprah Winfrey Show 18 times. Helen was installed in the Women’s Hall Of Fame for her leadership in the global women’s movement. Not only have they been married for 30 years, but they also know how to save marriages and help couples build the relationship of their dreams based on mutual respect and deeper communication.

Bickering with your partner and endless fights with your colleagues isn’t just a sign of one unhealthy relationship. It’s a problem with our culture.

Negative relationships have HUGE long-term impacts on society…

* 100% of people in a negative relationship have a greater risk of cardiac death.
* People in broken marriages are 60% less productive.
* Negativity in relationships is one of the contributing factors to at least nine major social issues — everything from substance addiction to violence and crime.

EVERYONE needs to watch this incredible interview and learn these life-changing — and surprisingly simple — communication techniques that’ll transform your relationships and your life.
