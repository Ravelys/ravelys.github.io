When a Chinese mother arrives in Melbourne to find her missing daughter, she enlists the help of Ashley Hudson. Inspired by court transcripts and actual events, The Jammed is a social thriller about trafficking and the sex slave trade in Melbourne.

As the story unravels the sinister workings of illegal prostitution and governmental deportation is filled with twists and surprises.
