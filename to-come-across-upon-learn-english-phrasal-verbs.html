Learn these two common phrasal verbs in English. "To come across" and "to come upon". English phrasal verbs can be difficult but they are very important to learn because they will help you to sound more like a native speaker and reach a higher level of fluency!

To come across and to come upon both have a similar meaning! Watch this short video to learn how to use these two phrasal verbs!
