DT Swiss makes some of the best hubs in the mountain bike world, plain and simple. So when they reached out for us to custom build a UNNO Dash spec’d with their brand new oil slick 240 EXP hubs, EX511 rims, and F535 fork, we knew a dream build video was needed.
