How To Socialize With People: In this video, you will learn SIX EASY STEPS to becoming more social with people and improving at socializing.

For example, one of the steps you should implement is to treat every social interaction you get into from here on out as an "experiment". By doing this, you lower the stakes of the interaction in a way that benefits you. Thinking of each social interaction as an experiment means you will not surprised or disappointed by failures, and make it more likely for you to try again.

Being social isn’t necessarily something you’re born with. Becoming more social and better at socializing is a matter of making it into a habit. Once it becomes a habit, being more social will become your default state.
