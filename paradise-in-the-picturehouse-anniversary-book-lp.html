Available from https://thestunning.net/store. The 30th anniversary edition of this classic, multi-platinum, Irish rock album is reissued on vinyl for the first time since 1990. Lovingly written by Steve and Joe, it features the story of the making of the album with never-before-seen photos and the inspiration behind each of the tracks. The guitar and piano arrangements are easy to follow with handwritten notes and playing tips written by the band themselves.
