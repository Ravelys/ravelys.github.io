Being able to bring the card the to the top of the deck is a necessary skill as a magician. There are many methods from this beginner card control through to really well executed passes, which when done well, are as good as invisible. Many card magicians view having a good ‘pass’, in their arsenal of card sleights, as a sign that they have reached a very competent level. A seamless ‘pass’ is hard to do.
