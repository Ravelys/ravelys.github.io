#Quantity #mywordbook

Meaning of Quantity : the fact of being measurable

Examples:
1. Is it available in sufficient quantity?
2. Mathematics is the science of pure quantity.
3. You must eliminate an unknown quantity.
4. The store owner checked the quantity of tires and realized he needed to order more.
5. Prices vary according to the quantity ordered.
