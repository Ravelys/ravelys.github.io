Welcome to the fifth video of our English study series.
In this one we would like to tell you more about our student city, student life, culture, food, free time activities, possibilities after graduation, study materials and so much more.

When searching for university studies finding the right place, you will like and enjoy is very important as it will be your new home for several years and you will form very important relationships and connections, professional and personal too. So hopefully, this video will provide some useful insights and help you to know the place better before you come.

Also in this video we will take you around our Educational Center, which is located in our University Hospital. So you can see the lecture hall, seminar rooms and also study rooms and chill out zones.

We still have one more video in this series that will introduce our student organizations and their activities. So do not miss that one out.
