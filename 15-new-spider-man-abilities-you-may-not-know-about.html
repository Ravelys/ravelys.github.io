The gist of the speech was that being a true hero is about more than just having a really awesome super suit with tons of gadgets. It’s kind of a strange thing for Iron Man to take a stance on, all things considered, but we got the message. Still, there’s no denying that there are some really awesome features incorporated in Spidey’s outfits. Today, we’re going to talk about some of the features and abilities they have that you may not have noticed.
