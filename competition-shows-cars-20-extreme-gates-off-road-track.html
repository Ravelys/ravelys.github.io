Competition Shows Cars – 20 Extreme GATES OFF Road Track. The last episode of the competition shows along the extreme off road track. As a result, the first place goes to the Ford Raptor - Axial SCX10 III, the second place - Traxxas TRX4 Sport and the third place - the Jeep Wrangler Rubicon - Axial SCX10 II.
