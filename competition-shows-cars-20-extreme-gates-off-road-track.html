Competition Shows Cars – 20 Extreme GATES OFF Road Track. The last episode of the competition shows along the extreme off road track. The entire track consists of 20 gates, from very simple to extremely difficult. Perhaps the most difficult is gate 10. It was impossible to get through these gates without a winch or roll- overs. As a result, the first place goes to the Ford Raptor - Axial SCX10 III, the second place - Traxxas TRX4 Sport and the third place - the Jeep Wrangler Rubicon - Axial SCX10 II.
