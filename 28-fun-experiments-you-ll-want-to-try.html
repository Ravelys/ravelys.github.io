Science is so much fun! And we have the experiments to prove it. Today’s video is all about exciting science experiments that will entertain you for hours and teach so many things about chemical reactions, viscosity, magnetic properties, temperature, and more. You’ll need soda cans, coins, and a magnet. You’ll also see an experiment that shows the effect of fluid viscosity on magnetic properties. You’ll need 3 fluids of different viscosity: water, oil, and syrup. Keep watching for a cold water vs. warm water experiment and more!
