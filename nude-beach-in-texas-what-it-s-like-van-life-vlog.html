We are Nat & Abi, a married couple from the USA, and we turned an ex prison van into an off grid cabin on wheels in order to travel the world with our 2 rescue dogs, Bear + Peluche. For the past 2 and half years, we’ve embraced life on the road in our tiny home and we share our highs + lows right here every week.
