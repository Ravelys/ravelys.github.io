We are Nat & Abi, a married couple from the USA, and we turned an ex prison van into an off grid cabin on wheels in order to travel the world with our 2 rescue dogs, Bear + Peluche. For the past 2 and half years, we’ve embraced life on the road in our tiny home and we share our highs + lows right here every week. By living and sharing our story, we hope to inspire you in your own life. Subscribe to our channel, and together, let’s play ride and seek.
