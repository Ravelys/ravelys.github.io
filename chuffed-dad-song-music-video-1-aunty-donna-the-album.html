Here it is! The first single from ‘Aunty Donna - The Album’! In this music video we see Broden Kelly (Manbeast in 1999, Benedick in Much Ado About Nothing) take on the role of Dad. Dad is a father who likes to sing songs about all the things that make him happy or chuffed. From pies to nice jeans to not watching south park, find out all the things Dad likes.
