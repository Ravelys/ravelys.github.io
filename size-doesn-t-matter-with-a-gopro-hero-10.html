Yappy Beeman vs Yellow Jacket Wasp infestation in an apartment using a brand new GoPro Hero 10 Black.

Welcome friends to my little corner of fun. And thank you for taking the time to read a little about this video in the description.
So, as I mentioned in the video, this is a return to a location near Birmingham Alabama that I have removed bees from in the past spring. A real nice complex and great folks to deal with. A resident came home to find they had some flying bugs inside their apartment. The assumed at that time they were honey bees. They contacted me to come evaluate and remove them.
This time of year in the south, things are actually wrapping up as far as bees go. Finding bee swarms or infestations is very minimal. However, its not as odd to find Yellow Jacket nest. To the general public its very hard to tell the difference. In either case, I am always ready to remove either one.
So lets get to the reality of the story. This is actually probably the smallest colony of yellow Jackets I have ever removed. I actually didn’t find the bulk of the nest until later through the removal. They were building directly on top of the drywall porch ceiling and when it dropped, I didn’t see it.
Although these little buggers can be mean as the devil, they fascinate me to work with. They are so much like a honey bee in their actions but have a completely different purpose in this world. I will be adding info about them in future videos.
Thank you again for stopping by and checking out my channel. Every view, comment, like and share always humbles me with the feedback.
Stay safe out there wherever you roam and my you be blessed through the holiday season.
