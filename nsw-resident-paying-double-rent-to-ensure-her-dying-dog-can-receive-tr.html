A border resident is paying a huge price to make the most of her dying pet’s final days.

Michelle Bryant has been forced to make a series of heartbreaking decisions, paying rent in NSW and Queensland to ensure her sick dog Taffy can receive cancer treatment on the Gold Coast.

"She could have minutes, days, I don’t really know, she’s very sick."
