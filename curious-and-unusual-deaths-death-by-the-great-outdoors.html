Death by the Great Outdoors

Mother Nature isn’t always nurturing and she was out to get an overly ambitious kite flyer. But a snake shouldn’t kill after it’s pickled in beer. And golf isn’t a particularly lethal sport.

This is a scientific investigation of case studies where death has come from unexpected means. The viewer will invest themselves into the story and the science without simply waiting for the next gruesome grizzly scene. And for these reasons Curious and Unusual Deaths will never feature any deaths involving children pets or suicides. Curious and Unusual Deaths is a romp through the world’s weirdest fatalities - and the scientific story behind each one. It’s the science that kills when death shows no mercy.
