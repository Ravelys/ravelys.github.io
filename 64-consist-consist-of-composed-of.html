consist of - ~으로 구성되다
be composed of - ~로 구성되어 있다
comprise - ~을 구성하다
constitute - ~을 구성하다
make up - ~을 이루다

Consist of [예문]
1. The committee consists of ten members.
2. North America consists of three countries: the USA, Canada, and Mexico.

be composed of [예문]
1. All living things are composed of cells. 
2. She composed a letter of protest.
COMPOSE : 작성하다

Comprise [예문]
be composed of = be comprised with =그러나 권장 X

1. The new board will comprise twelve members.
2. Seoul comprises more than 15 percent of the entire Korean population.
