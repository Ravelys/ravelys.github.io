What makes Malaysia beautiful? Is it the diverse cultures embracing each other in unity or the myriad of faiths and traditions that sets her apart? ‘Diversity’ depicts the minute details and moments that makes you feel like you’re experiencing it yourself.

Directed by world-renowned TV commercial director, Mark Toia, these videos and sounds encapsulate the essence of collaboration by using a wide variety of instruments and production techniques by Chilli Pepper Films. ‘Diversity’ is a collaboration between Tourism Malaysia and Sharp Electronics (M) Sdn Bhd.
