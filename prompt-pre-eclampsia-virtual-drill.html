In this PROMPT virtual drill, you can watch a team managing severe pre-eclampsia and eclampsia. There are pauses throughout to reflect on how to manage an emergency like this in your own maternity unit.

Your team can then feedback how this team managed in terms of:
• communication
• situational awareness
• team roles and leadership
• the clinical management
