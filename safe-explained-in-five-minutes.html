This video explains SAFe 5.0 in five minutes (and 29 seconds). It illustrates how the SAFe methodology uses cadences, ceremonies, and processes applicable to teams, programs, large solutions, and portfolios. You’ll also learn how SAFe integrates Lean-Agile principles at scale, and how using the Framework can help you build an agile business.

About Scaled Agile, Inc.
The Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe®), created by Scaled Agile, Inc., is the #1 framework for business agility, with proven benefits in productivity, time-to-market, quality, and employee engagement.
