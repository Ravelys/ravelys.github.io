When it comes to health, humans and animals are closely linked. They share the same environment, and can suffer from the same diseases, too. A new center at UC San Diego seeks to improve the health of both species. KPBS Health Reporter Kenny Goldberg tells us the center encourages close collaborations between veterinarians from UC Davis and human specialists from San Diego.
