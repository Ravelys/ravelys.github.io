Let’s get something clear – Jesus was Middle Eastern. Did he have lighter skin or darker skin for someone in the Middle East? Nobody knows, but we can make a guess that he probably looked, you guessed it, Middle Eastern. The details don’t matter. What matters is what he did and who he was, not what color skin he had.
