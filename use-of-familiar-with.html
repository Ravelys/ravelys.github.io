ABOUT THIS VIDEO:

This video is about the following situation in HIndi:

Main tumse parichit hun.
Tum is business se parichat nahi ho.

STRUCTURE:
POSITIVE SENTENCE

[Sub + is/am/are/was/were/will be + familiar with + Ob/verb+ing]

Examples:
Main tumse parichit hun.
I am familiar with you.

wah jmere business se parichit hai.
He is familiar with my business.

Main in niyamo se parichit nahi tha.
I was not familiar with these rules.

Tum use bahut jald parichit ho jaoge.
You will be familiar with him very soon.

NEGATIVE SENTENCE
[Sub + H. V. + not + familiar with + Ob]

examples:
Main tumse parichit nahi hun.
I am not familiar with you.

Wah mere doston se parichit nahi hai.
He is not familiar with my friends.

INTERROGATIVE SENTNCE
[H.V. + sub + familiar with + Ob]

examples:
Kya tum usse parichit ho?
Are you familiar with him?

Kya tum is software se parichit ho?
Are you familiar with this software?
